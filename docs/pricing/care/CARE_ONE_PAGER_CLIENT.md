## What Vantus Care is

Vantus Care is the recurring support layer that follows project delivery.
It is designed for clients who want ownership, visibility, and a predictable support rhythm after launch.

## What makes Care different

- You keep ownership of accounts, data, hardware, and credentials.
- Pricing is transparent and structured.
- Security and documentation are part of the operating baseline.
- Recurring support stays separate from project work.

## Care tiers

### Foundation

For smaller environments that need dependable day-to-day support.

### Advanced

For growing environments that need stronger security, reporting, and planning depth.

### Sovereign

For higher-governance environments that need strategic support and a more structured operating rhythm.

## Starting prices

| Tier       |        Starting price | Minimum monthly |

## Common add-ons

- server management
- application server management
- database server management
- after-hours coverage
- compliance evidence support
- hardware procurement assistance

## What Care does not include by default

- major project work without written scope
- migrations and custom builds under recurring support
- hardware leasing or device financing
- legal, audit, or certification guarantees

## Service boundary rule

If the work needs new scope, significant change, or non-routine delivery effort, it moves into a scoped project or change request.

## Best fit

Vantus Care is best for organizations that want:

- a support model built around ownership,
- clearer reporting and accountability,
- stronger documentation and handoff discipline,
- recurring support without MSP lock-in.
