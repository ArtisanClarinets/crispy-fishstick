# CHK-CARE-014: Quarterly Governance Review Checklist

**Document ID:** VS-CARE-CHK-014  
**Version:** 1.0.0  
**Effective Date:** 2026-02-21  
**Owner:** Account Manager  
**Status:** Active  
**Reading Level:** 8th Grade  
**Estimated Time:** 6-8 hours (prep + meeting + follow-up)  
**Risk Rating:** High (missed reviews lead to misalignment and surprise costs)

**Goal:** Conduct strategic quarterly reviews that align IT initiatives with business priorities, refresh roadmaps, and proactively address risks before they become incidents.

---

## 1. Pre-Meeting Preparation (Week Before)

- [ ] Meeting scheduled with key stakeholders (calendar invites sent)
- [ ] Agenda distributed (topics, time allocations, pre-reads)
- [ ] Quarterly review document initiated (TPL-CARE-004 template)
- [ ] Previous quarter's action items gathered (status review)
- [ ] Last quarter's scorecards compiled (3 months of data)
- [ ] Risk register updated (current risks, new items, closed items)
- [ ] Roadmap status collected (what was planned vs. delivered)

**Time Estimate:** 2-3 hours  
**CMMC Alignment:** CA.2.1 (security assessments), CA.3.1 (system audits)

---

## 2. Quarter-in-Review Analysis

- [ ] Wins celebrated (achievements, improvements delivered)
- [ ] Misses acknowledged (what didn't happen, why)
- [ ] Incident patterns reviewed (recurring issues, root causes)
- [ ] SLA performance analyzed (met vs. missed targets)
- [ ] Budget variance checked (actual vs. planned spend)
- [ ] Scope changes noted (additions, removals, modifications)
- [ ] Client satisfaction summary included (feedback, surveys)

**Time Estimate:** 30-45 minutes  
**Vantus standard:** requiring pattern analysis across quarter

---

## 3. Risk Register Review

- [ ] Current risks reviewed (likelihood, impact, trends)
- [ ] New risks identified (emerging threats, business changes)
- [ ] Closed risks confirmed (mitigation verified effective)
- [ ] Risk owners verified (still appropriate, contact current)
- [ ] Mitigation status checked (actions on track)
- [ ] Risk appetite discussion (accept, transfer, mitigate)
- [ ] Documentation updated (TPL-CARE-013 current)

**Time Estimate:** 20-30 minutes  
**Owner:** Security Lead (security risks), Account Manager (business risks)

---

## 4. Lifecycle & Refresh Planning

- [ ] Warranty expirations checked (next 120 days)
- [ ] End-of-life announcements reviewed (vendor notices)
- [ ] Asset age analysis completed (items past refresh horizon)
- [ ] Replacement options researched (good/better/best)
- [ ] Budget estimates prepared (costs, timing)
- [ ] Priorities ranked (urgency vs. budget constraints)
- [ ] Decisions captured (approve, defer, decline)

**Time Estimate:** 30-45 minutes  
**Related SOP:** SOP-CARE-024 (Lifecycle Planning)

---

## 5. Roadmap Refresh

- [ ] Previous 90-day roadmap reviewed (what was achieved)
- [ ] Business priorities confirmed (what matters now)
- [ ] IT initiatives proposed (alignment with business goals)
- [ ] Dependencies mapped (what must happen first)
- [ ] Resource constraints identified (budget, time, expertise)
- [ ] New 90-day roadmap drafted (prioritized initiatives)
- [ ] Owners assigned (who does what)
- [ ] Timeline committed (when milestones occur)

**Time Estimate:** 45-60 minutes

---

## 6. Meeting Facilitation

- [ ] Participants introduced (roles, attendance)
- [ ] Agenda confirmed (any additions or adjustments)
- [ ] Previous minutes reviewed (actions from last QBR)
- [ ] Each agenda item covered (time managed)
- [ ] Decisions documented (what was decided, by whom)
- [ ] Action items captured (what, who, when)
- [ ] Next meeting scheduled (date confirmed)

**Time Estimate:** 60-90 minutes (meeting duration)  
**CMMC Alignment:** CA.2.2 (vulnerability scans), CA.3.2 (remediation progress)

---

## 7. Documentation & Distribution

- [ ] Meeting notes finalized (within 48 hours)
- [ ] Decisions log updated (TPL-CARE-014)
- [ ] Action items entered in tracking system
- [ ] Roadmap published (shared with all stakeholders)
- [ ] Quarterly review document completed (TPL-CARE-004)
- [ ] Evidence pack updated (documents archived)
- [ ] Distribution completed (all attendees receive notes)

**Time Estimate:** 1-2 hours

---

## 8. Follow-Through

- [ ] Action items tracked weekly (progress monitoring)
- [ ] Blockers escalated promptly (don't let items stall)
- [ ] Roadmap milestones monitored (early warning if slipping)
- [ ] Budget items processed (procurement initiated)
- [ ] Decisions implemented (follow-through on commitments)
- [ ] Next quarter pre-work started (early preparation)

**Time Estimate:** Ongoing (throughout quarter)

## Success Criteria
