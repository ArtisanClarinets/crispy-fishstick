# CHK-CARE-019: Training & Drill Checklist

**Document ID:** VS-CARE-CHK-019  
**Version:** 1.0.0  
**Effective Date:** 2026-02-21  
**Owner:** Account Manager  
**Status:** Active  
**Reading Level:** 8th Grade  
**Estimated Time:** 4-8 hours (per training event or drill)  
**Risk Rating:** Medium (untrained staff = higher incident impact)

**Goal:** Deliver effective, non-punitive training and realistic drills that build practical skills, improve response readiness, and generate actionable improvements.

---

## 1. Training Needs Assessment

- [ ] Audience identified (who needs training?)
- [ ] Current skill level assessed (what do they know now?)
- [ ] Gap analysis completed (what's missing?)
- [ ] Business risks considered (what could go wrong?)
- [ ] Learning objectives defined (what will they know/do after?)
- [ ] Success criteria established (how will we know it worked?)

**Time Estimate:** 30-45 minutes  
**Owner:** Account Manager + Security Lead  
**CMMC Alignment:** AT.2.1 (security awareness), AT.2.2 (training records)

---

## 2. Training Design

- [ ] Format selected:
  - **Live Workshop:** Interactive skills (best for complex topics)
  - **Tabletop Exercise:** Decision-making practice (SOP-CARE-027)
  - **Online Module:** Awareness topics (self-paced)
  - **Hands-On Drill:** Technical skills (practical application)
- [ ] Content outline created (topics and sequence)
- [ ] Materials prepared (slides, handouts, scenarios)
- [ ] Plain language verified (8th grade reading level)
- [ ] Real examples included (relevant to their business)
- [ ] Duration appropriate (attention span considered)

**Time Estimate:** 1-2 hours  
**Vantus standard:** requiring plain language and real examples

---

## 3. Logistics Planning

- [ ] Date/time scheduled (minimal business disruption)
- [ ] Location/virtual platform confirmed
- [ ] Participants invited (calendar invites sent)
- [ ] Prerequisites communicated (what to prepare/bring)
- [ ] Materials printed/uploaded (ready for session)
- [ ] Technology tested (A/V, screen sharing, recording)
- [ ] Backup plan prepared (what if tech fails?)

**Time Estimate:** 30-45 minutes

---

## 4. Pre-Drill Preparation (for exercises)

- [ ] Scenario developed (realistic, relevant situation)
- [ ] Facilitator guide created (prompts, timing, questions)
- [ ] Roles assigned (who plays what in scenario)
- [ ] Injects prepared (new information to introduce during drill)
- [ ] Success metrics defined (what good looks like)
- [ ] Safety rules established (no real system changes)
- [ ] Current procedures reviewed (what should they know)

**Time Estimate:** 1-2 hours  
**Related:** CHK-CARE-007 (Simulation Safety) for technical drills

---

## 5. Session Delivery

- [ ] Welcome and objectives stated (what we'll cover)
- [ ] Non-punitive tone set (learning, not blame)
- [ ] Participation encouraged (questions welcomed)
- [ ] Content delivered at appropriate pace
- [ ] Examples made relevant (their systems, their risks)
- [ ] Engagement maintained (interactive elements)
- [ ] Time managed (covers all topics)

**Time Estimate:** 1-3 hours (varies by topic)

---

## 6. Exercise Facilitation (for drills/tabletops)

- [ ] Scenario presented clearly (set the situation)
- [ ] Questions posed to drive decisions (what would you do?)
- [ ] Decisions documented (what they chose)
- [ ] Time pressure applied (simulate real urgency)
- [ ] Injects delivered (new information, escalating situation)
- [ ] Gaps identified (what they didn't know)
- [ ] Confusion points noted (what was unclear)

**Time Estimate:** 60-90 minutes (typical tabletop)

---

## 7. Outcome Capture

- [ ] Attendance recorded (who participated)
- [ ] Key learnings documented (what they learned)
- [ ] Questions raised captured (what they asked)
- [ ] Improvement opportunities noted (what could be better)
- [ ] Engagement assessed (were they involved?)
- [ ] Knowledge check performed (did they learn it?)

**Time Estimate:** 15-30 minutes

---

## 8. After-Action Reporting

- [ ] After-Action Report written (SOP-CARE-027)
  - Scenario summary (what we practiced)
  - Participants list (who attended)
  - Key findings (what worked, what didn't)
  - Gaps identified (what's missing)
  - Recommended actions (specific improvements)
- [ ] Report distributed (within 48 hours)
- [ ] Findings presented to leadership (if significant)

**Time Estimate:** 30-60 minutes  
**CMMC Alignment:** IR.1.3 (incident response testing), IR.2.2 (incident response plan)

---

## 9. Improvement Implementation

- [ ] Action items created (from identified gaps)
- [ ] Owners assigned (who does what)
- [ ] Due dates set (when will it be done)
- [ ] Priority established (critical vs. nice-to-have)
- [ ] Progress tracked (regular status checks)
- [ ] Completion verified (did it get done)

**Time Estimate:** Ongoing (until all items closed)

---

## 10. Documentation & Reporting

- [ ] Training records updated (who completed what)
- [ ] Skill levels recorded (competency tracking)
- [ ] Monthly scorecard updated (training metrics)
- [ ] Quarterly review inclusion (training status)
- [ ] Next training scheduled (follow-up planned)
- [ ] Effectiveness measured (did behavior change?)

**Time Estimate:** 30 minutes

---

## Non-Punitive Culture Checklist

Throughout training and drills:

- [ ] **No blame** for mistakes ("What can we improve?" not "Who messed up?")
- [ ] **Questions welcomed** (no such thing as "stupid question")
- [ ] **Improvement rewarded** (recognition for growth)
- [ ] **Fear eliminated** (safe environment for learning)
- [ ] **Success celebrated** (acknowledge what went well)

---

## Training Types and Frequency

| Training Type | Audience | Frequency | Format |
|---------------|----------|-----------|--------|
| Security Awareness | All staff | Annual | Online + Quiz |
| Phishing Recognition | All staff | Quarterly | Simulated tests |
| Incident Response | IT staff | Semi-annual | Tabletop drill |
| Ransomware Response | Leadership | Annual | Tabletop drill |
| Business Continuity | Key staff | Annual | Tabletop drill |
| Technical Skills | IT staff | As needed | Hands-on |
| New Hire Onboarding | New employees | Within 30 days | Workshop |

## Success Criteria

| Criterion | Target | Verification |
|-----------|--------|--------------|
| Completion rate | 95%+ | Attendance records |
| Knowledge retention | 80%+ pass rate | Post-training quiz |
| Behavior change | Incidents reduced | Incident analysis |
| Participant satisfaction | 90%+ | Post-training survey |
| Improvement implementation | 90% actions closed | Backlog review |
