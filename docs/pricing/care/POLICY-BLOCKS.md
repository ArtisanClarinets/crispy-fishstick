## No Hardware Subscriptions

Vantus Care is a service membership. Vantus does not lease hardware or bundle equipment into monthly payments.  
If Vantus sources hardware, it is purchased outright by the client. Care fees are service-only.

## Proof / Metrics Discipline

When we report a metric, we define it, state the time window, and identify the source system.  
If a claim cannot be evidenced, we do not publish it.

## Authorized Simulation (If Contracted)

Any authorized adversary simulation requires written authorization, a signed Rules of Engagement (ROE) pack, explicit exclusions, stop authority, and safety constraints.  
We report findings and remediation recommendations; we do not provide unlawful or harmful instructions.

## Compliance Support Disclaimer

We can help map controls and assemble evidence artifacts, but we do not provide legal advice or guarantee audit outcomes.

## Ownership-First Positioning

**Full Ownership and Control:** You own your accounts, your data, and your hardware. Vantus provides ownership-based support: we guide and execute, while you retain control of the environment.

**Client-Owned, Exit-Ready:** Care is designed around client-owned infrastructure and documented handoff readiness. If you transition providers, the goal is a cleaner handoff with less friction.

**Vendor-Neutral Architecture:** We design and maintain systems to reduce proprietary lock-in where practical. The goal is to keep systems portable and easier to hand off.

## Client Experience Covenant (Small Business Focus)

Vantus Care is designed for small-business operators who need clarity, speed, and ownership without unnecessary technical complexity.
**1. The "No Jargon" Mandate:** We communicate in plain language (targeted at an 8th-grade reading level). We do not hide behind acronyms; we explain risks and actions in business terms.
**2. Interface Simplicity:** Client-facing views should use plain status signals and straightforward next steps suited to non-technical users.
**3. Client-Centric Workflows:** Approvals, access reviews, and asset tracking should be presented clearly so the client remains the final authority.
