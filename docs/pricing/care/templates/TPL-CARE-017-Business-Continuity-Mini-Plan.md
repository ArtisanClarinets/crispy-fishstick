A simple, practical plan for what to do when things go wrong—internet outages, server failures, cyber attacks, or disasters. Keeps your business running when technology fails.

## Quick Reference: First 30 Minutes

When disaster strikes:

1. **Assess** (0-5 minutes)
   - What's happening?
   - Who's affected?
   - Is anyone in danger?

2. **Notify** (5-15 minutes)
   - Call plan owner
   - Contact key people
   - Alert staff if needed

3. **Activate** (15-30 minutes)
   - Start scenario response
   - Implement workarounds
   - Begin recovery

4. **Communicate** (Ongoing)
   - Keep people informed
   - Document actions
   - Update regularly
