# GAP_ANALYSIS - Vantus Care Documentation Suite

**Version:** 1.2.0
**Last updated:** 2026-03-07
**Classification:** Internal - remediation and maintenance record
**Publication rule:** Internal reference only.
**Status:** Major structural issues addressed; maintain through periodic review and source validation

## Executive assessment

The Care suite is now organized around a clearer, more maintainable structure, but the remaining risk is legacy drift inside older SOPs and templates.
The highest-priority issues were pricing-source confusion, tier-name inconsistency, stale "100% complete" claims, and weak alignment to the enterprise offer ladder.

## What was corrected at the suite level

- Established a canonical Care public service catalog.
- Established a canonical public pricing document and add-on matrix.
- Established a canonical build-to-care crosswalk.
- Reframed Care as the recurring support layer that follows project delivery.
- Removed suite-level claims that the package was already perfect or fully verified.
- Standardized top-level package language around `Foundation`, `Advanced`, and `Sovereign`.

## Remaining remediation areas

### High

- External publication of benchmark or valuation materials still requires dated source validation and citation support.
- Commercial templates should continue to evolve as Vantus learns from live deals.

### Medium

- Supporting documents should continue moving from remediation language toward long-term operating-language consistency.
- Periodic link, taxonomy, and publication-safety review should remain part of suite maintenance.

## Target state

A final-quality Care suite should keep four layers in sync:

1. Public offer and positioning
2. Commercial scope, SLA, and pricing governance
3. Internal SOPs, templates, and checklists
4. Evidence, reporting, and compliance-support boundaries

## Definition of done for final cleanup

- Only active tier names appear in current Care materials.
- Public claims are evidence-backed and publication-safe.
- Pricing, quoting, and add-on logic map to the same source documents.
- Cross-links resolve to the current `care/` structure.
- Enterprise-pack strategy and Care packaging tell one coherent story.

## Recommended next maintenance sequence

1. Re-validate any external-facing claims with dated sources before publication.
2. Expand commercial templates only when live sales process needs justify it.
3. Run a quarterly taxonomy, link, and publication-safety audit across the full suite.
