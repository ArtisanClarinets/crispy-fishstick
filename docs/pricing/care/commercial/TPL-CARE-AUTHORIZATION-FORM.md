## Authorized recurring services

The Client authorizes Vantus to provide the recurring services listed in the approved service order.
Vantus is authorized to perform recurring operational support activities within the contracted scope, including administration, support coordination, vendor coordination, reporting, and standard service requests within agreed limits.

## Ownership and access acknowledgement

The Client acknowledges and agrees:

- the Client retains ownership of systems, accounts, data, licenses, hardware, and credentials,
- Vantus is authorized to access agreed systems only for support delivery,
- Vantus does not lease, rent, finance, or bundle hardware to the Client,
- access rights may be limited to what is reasonably required to deliver the contracted recurring services.

## Out-of-scope work acknowledgement

The Client understands that the following are outside recurring scope unless separately approved in writing:

- project work,
- migrations,
- custom development,
- major platform changes,
- major remediation,
- after-hours emergency work not already purchased,
- hardware procurement beyond agreed advisory or coordination support.

## Client approvals and dependencies

The Client will:

- provide accurate contacts and escalation paths,
- maintain required vendor licenses and third-party contracts,
- provide timely approvals and access,
- identify an internal decision-maker for support matters,
- notify Vantus of material environment changes that may affect scope or pricing.
