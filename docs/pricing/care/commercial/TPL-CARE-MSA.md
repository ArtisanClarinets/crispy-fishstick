# TPL-CARE-MSA

**Title:** Vantus Care Managed Services Agreement
**Status:** Baseline template
**Last updated:** 2026-03-07

## Purpose

This Agreement sets the baseline terms for Vantus Care recurring managed services.
It governs ongoing support after project delivery or an approved transition-in period.
It does not include one-time project work unless separately scoped.

## Parties and term

This Agreement is between Vantus and the Client identified in the applicable service order.
The service order defines the effective date, initial term, renewal structure, and billing cadence.

## Service model

Vantus Care is an ownership-based support model.
The Client retains ownership of its systems, accounts, credentials, data, hardware, licenses, and vendors.
Vantus provides recurring support, coordination, reporting, and operational guidance within the purchased scope.

## Recurring scope

Recurring services are limited to the package, service boundaries, response targets, and deliverables listed in the service order.
Typical recurring work may include administration, monitoring review, minor routine changes, vendor coordination, reporting, and operational planning.

## Out of scope

The following are out of scope unless separately approved in writing:

- project work,
- migrations,
- major redesigns,
- custom development,
- major remediation,
- hardware leasing or financing,
- legal, audit, or certification guarantees.

## Client responsibilities

The Client is responsible for:

- executive decisions and budget approval,
- required licenses and third-party contracts,
- timely access approvals,
- named contacts for business and technical decisions,
- final approval of changes that affect business risk, cost, or operations.

## Third-party services

Vantus may coordinate with Client vendors and platforms but does not control third-party service levels, product limitations, outages, or vendor fees.
Third-party costs remain the Client's responsibility unless expressly stated otherwise.

## Fees and billing

Recurring fees are billed as defined in the service order.
Out-of-scope work, overages, transition work, or special requests may be billed separately with Client approval.

## Change management

Material changes to service scope, support window, covered systems, or response expectations must be documented in an updated service order, addendum, or approved quote.

## Service levels

Service levels are operational targets described in the service order.
They are not guarantees, penalties, or credits unless expressly stated in writing.

## Data, access, and security

The Client owns its data at all times.
Vantus will use only the access reasonably required to perform services and will support orderly access transition at offboarding.

## Offboarding and transition rights

At termination or expiration, the Client may request reasonable transition assistance to return administration, documentation, and known operational context to the Client or its designee.
Additional post-termination transition effort may be separately billed if it exceeds the included offboarding scope.

## Suspension and termination

Either party may terminate for material breach not cured within the notice period defined in the service order or governing agreement.
Either party may elect not to renew by providing notice in the stated renewal window.

## No expanded relationship

Vantus is a service provider and not the Client's legal advisor, auditor, insurer, fiduciary, or hardware lessor.
