# SOP: Content Collection and Approval

**Version:** 1.0
**Last Updated:** 2026-03-05
**Owner:** Delivery Lead
**Audience:** Delivery Lead / Client Approver

---

## 1. Purpose
Collect complete content so the site can launch on time.

## 2. Who Does This
Delivery lead and the client approver.

## 3. Tools You Need
- Content Workbook (template)
- Shared folder for assets
- VSP-lite page tracker

## 4. Starting Requirements
Before you start, make sure:
- [ ] Client approver is named
- [ ] Page list is agreed

## 5. Step-by-Step Instructions

### Step 1: Send the workbook
1. Send the workbook to the client.
2. Explain the deadline.

**What you should see:** Client confirms receipt.

### Step 2: Track page status
1. Create a page row for each page.
2. Mark as draft/in review/approved.

**What you should see:** A status board.

### Step 3: Approve content
1. Client reviews each page.
2. Client marks “approved.”

**Warning:** No approval means no launch.

## 6. Quality Check
After finishing, verify:
- [ ] 100% pages approved
- [ ] Logos/photos are received
- [ ] Legal pages are provided (privacy, terms)

## 7. Common Problems and Fixes

| Problem | Why It Happens | How to Fix |
|---------|---------------|------------|
| Client delays | No owner | Escalate to decision maker |
| Missing photos | Disorganized assets | Provide a folder and naming rules |

## 8. Notes
**Assumptions made:**
- Vantus requires complete content before launch.

**Who to ask for help:** Founder
