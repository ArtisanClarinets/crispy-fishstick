# SOP: Sales Discovery Call

**Version:** 1.0
**Last Updated:** 2026-03-05
**Owner:** Founder
**Audience:** Founder / Sales

---

## 1. Purpose
Understand the real problem and scope the right package.

## 2. Who Does This
Founder.

## 3. Tools You Need
- Call notes template
- Content workbook
- VSP-lite CRM

## 4. Starting Requirements
Before you start, make sure:
- [ ] You reviewed their current website
- [ ] You have 3 screenshots of issues

## 5. Step-by-Step Instructions

### Step 1: Confirm the goal
1. Ask what the website must do.
2. Ask how they get customers today.

**What you should see:** A clear outcome (leads, calls, bookings).

### Step 2: Confirm constraints
1. Ask about timeline.
2. Ask who will approve content.

**Warning:** No approver means delays.

### Step 3: Choose the package
1. Recommend Website, Website+CMS, or Portal.
2. Explain why in plain language.

**What you should see:** Agreement on next step.

### Step 4: Set next action
1. Send content workbook.
2. Schedule kickoff or proposal review.

## 6. Quality Check
After finishing, verify:
- [ ] Package recommendation is recorded
- [ ] Approver is identified
- [ ] Next meeting is booked

## 7. Common Problems and Fixes

| Problem | Why It Happens | How to Fix |
|---------|---------------|------------|
| They want “everything” | No priorities | Ask for the #1 workflow only |
| They have no content | They are busy | Use the content workbook gate |

## 8. Notes
**Assumptions made:**
- We do not launch half-finished sites.

**Who to ask for help:** Founder
