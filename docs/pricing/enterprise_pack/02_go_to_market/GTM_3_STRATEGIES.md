## Your 3 Go-to-Market Strategies

> **Strategy:** Option A — Gulf Coast Leader
> Target: Gulf Coast SMBs (Ft. Walton, Pensacola, Mobile)
> Revenue: $500K Y1 → $1.4M Y2 → $2.2M Y3

Based on Vantus Systems' current stage (bootstrapped, premium-by-proof, targeting Gulf Coast SMBs with outdated websites), here are your 3 best go-to-market strategies:

---

### Strategy 1: “Website Reality Check” → Paid Build Pipeline (local outbound)

**The Strategy:**
Start with a zero-fluff diagnostic that instantly proves competence. Your ICP does not want marketing talk; they want to know what’s broken and what it costs to fix. The Reality Check creates authority and a natural next step into your productized package.

**The Exact Playbook:**

**Step 1:** Build a one-page Reality Check template (performance, a11y, SEO basics, ownership risks). Use screenshots and plain language.

**Step 2:** Create a target list of 100 local businesses with visibly outdated sites (Ft. Walton, Destin, Niceville, Pensacola). Prioritize high-value verticals.

**Step 3:** Produce 10 Reality Checks per week (60–90 minutes each max). Keep them short and brutal: “Here are the top 5 issues costing you leads.”

**Step 4:** Deliver via email + a 15-minute call offer. CTA: “We can rebuild this in 4–8 weeks with CMS and proof artifacts.”

**Metrics to Track:**

- Reality Checks produced/week
- Reply rate (goal: 15%+)
- Calls booked/week (goal: 3–5)
- Close rate (goal: 20–30%)

**Expected Milestones:**
Within 30 days: 40 checks → 12 calls → 3 deals.

**Do This Today:**
Pick 10 local businesses. Run Lighthouse + quick a11y check + identify “who owns your domain” risk. Draft the first 2 Reality Checks.

---

### Strategy 2: Proof-Artifact Content Engine (local SEO + trust compounding)

**The Strategy:**
Local competitors have portfolios. You will have **receipts**: before/after performance, accessibility improvements, SEO fixes, and deployment hardening. This builds authority and inbound.

**The Exact Playbook:**

**Step 1:** For every project, publish a “Proof Page” with:

- before/after screenshots,
- Lighthouse scores,
- accessibility checklist outcome,
- what changed and why.

**Step 2:** Create “location pages” (Ft. Walton, Pensacola, Destin) that are not spam — include real case studies and local references.

**Step 3:** Publish 1 deep-dive per week:

- “Why slow websites lose leads” (with local examples)
- “What WCAG means for small business”
- “How to know who owns your domain”

**Metrics to Track:**

- Organic impressions
- Calls booked from organic
- Google Business Profile views

**Expected Milestones:**
60–90 days: rank locally for “website rebuild [city]” + consistent inbound.

**Do This Today:**
Draft one Proof Page template and write the first article: “How to tell your website is costing you leads.”

---

### Strategy 3: Partner channel (CPAs, IT shops, print/sign companies)

**The Strategy:**
Many SMB decision makers trust their CPA, local IT, or vendor relationships. You become the backend engineering partner those vendors don’t have.

**The Exact Playbook:**

**Step 1:** Identify 20 partners: CPAs, managed IT providers, print/sign shops, marketing freelancers.

**Step 2:** Offer a simple value exchange:

- You do a Reality Check for their client.
- They get a co-branded “Fix Plan” they can share.
- You pay a referral fee or reciprocal work.

**Step 3:** Host a monthly “Small Business Tech Ownership” breakfast session.

**Metrics to Track:**

- Partner meetings/week
- Partner referrals/month
- Close rate on partner leads

**Expected Milestones:**
90 days: 3 active partners generating consistent leads.

**Do This Today:**
Write a 10-sentence partner pitch and send it to 3 local CPAs.

---

## Execution Priority

**Start with:** Strategy 1 — Reality Check outbound.

**Why this order:** Strategy 1 creates immediate pipeline with minimal cost. Strategy 2 compounds trust and reduces future outbound dependence. Strategy 3 becomes stable once you have 2–3 case studies and proof pages to arm partners.

---

## Success Criteria

You'll know these strategies are working when:

- 40 Reality Checks delivered and 10+ calls booked in 30 days.
- 3 published proof pages and 10 five-star Google reviews within 60 days.
- 3 active referral partners sending at least 2 leads/month by day 90.
