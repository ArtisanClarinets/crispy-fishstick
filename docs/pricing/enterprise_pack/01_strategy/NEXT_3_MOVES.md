## Your 3 Next Moves

> **Strategy:** Option A — Gulf Coast Leader
> Target: Gulf Coast SMBs (Ft. Walton, Pensacola, Mobile)

Based on Vantus Systems' current situation (bootstrapped, high-quality delivery, targeting Gulf Coast SMB website modernizations), here are your 3 highest-impact next moves:

---

### Move 1: Turn the “Website Reality Check” into a weekly pipeline machine

**The Strategy:**
Your bottleneck is lead flow, not capability. A repeatable Reality Check outbound cadence creates pipeline without spending money, and it positions you as the adult in the room.

**The Exact Playbook:**

**Step 1:** Finalize the Reality Check template (see `../07_templates/TEMPLATE-Website-Reality-Check.md`).

**Step 2:** Build a target list of 100 businesses with visibly outdated sites (prioritize high-value verticals).

**Step 3:** Produce 10 Reality Checks/week (time-box each to 60–90 minutes).

**Step 4:** Send a short email: 3 issues, 1 screenshot, 1 CTA for a 15-minute call.

**Metrics to Track:**

- Reality Checks sent/week
- Reply rate
- Calls booked

**Expected Results:**
3–5 calls booked within 14 days.

**Do This Today:**
Pick 10 businesses and produce the first 2 Reality Checks.

---

### Move 2: Build Vantus Seed (your delivery multiplier)

**The Strategy:**
If every project starts from scratch, you will never scale. Vantus Seed is how you keep quality high while reducing cycle time.

**The Exact Playbook:**

**Step 1:** Create a baseline repo with SEO, accessibility scaffolding, and deployment templates.

**Step 2:** Add a CMS module with a default content model.

**Step 3:** Add a proof-artifact script checklist (Lighthouse/a11y/security headers).

**Metrics to Track:**

- Hours saved per project
- QA gate pass rate on first attempt

**Expected Results:**
20% cycle time reduction by project #5.

**Do This Today:**
Create the repo skeleton and add the non-negotiable quality gates.

---

### Move 3: Publish proof pages and collect reviews as a system

**The Strategy:**
Local markets run on trust. Proof pages + reviews turn your work into a compounding asset.

**The Exact Playbook:**

**Step 1:** Create a Proof Page template (before/after, metrics, what changed).

**Step 2:** After each launch, publish the proof page within 72 hours.

**Step 3:** Request a review using a single email template and make it easy.

**Metrics to Track:**

- Proof pages published/month
- Reviews/month

**Expected Results:**
10 five-star reviews by day 90.

**Do This Today:**
Draft the review request email and publish the first proof page template.

---

## Execution Priority

**Start with:** Move 1 — Reality Check pipeline.

**Why this order:** You need pipeline now. While outbound runs, build Vantus Seed in parallel (Move 2). Proof pages and reviews (Move 3) are what make future sales easier and cheaper.

---

## Success Criteria

You'll know these moves are working when:

- 40 Reality Checks delivered and 10 calls booked in 30 days.
- Vantus Seed reduces delivery cycle time by 20% by project #5.
- 10 five-star reviews and 3 proof pages are published within 90 days.
