# VANTUS_CARE_ALIGNMENT

**Version:** 1.1.0
**Last Updated:** 2026-03-08
**Status:** Active

Align Vantus Care with the primary enterprise-pack offer ladder.

## Core rule

Vantus Care is not the first sale.
The first sale is usually a project outcome from the core ladder:

1. Website Rebuild
2. Website + CMS
3. Website + Business Portal

Care is the recurring support and governance layer that follows delivery when the client needs continuity, reporting, and operational discipline.

## Why this matters

This keeps Vantus aligned with the founder context:

- lead with productized builds,
- prove quality with shipped work,
- then expand into recurring support where it is justified.

## Positioning guidance

### Build offer message

- enterprise-grade website and system delivery,
- ownership and handoff,
- proof artifacts,
- clear scope and change control.

### Care message

- recurring support for systems you own,
- governance and reporting after launch,
- structured security and operational hygiene,
- no MSP lock-in and no hardware leasing.

## Mapping guidance

| Core offer                | Typical Care follow-on |
| ------------------------- | ---------------------- |
| Website Rebuild           | Foundation             |
| Website + CMS             | Advanced               |
| Website + Business Portal | Sovereign              |

Use `../../care/CARE_BUILD_TO_CARE_CROSSWALK.md` for the operational detail.

## Commercial guardrails

- Do not let Care hide unresolved project scope.
- Do not quote recurring support before launch artifacts and ownership handoff are clear.
- Keep recurring support pricing separate from build pricing.
- Use Care when the client needs continuity, not just a one-time fix.
