# SOP: Lead Qualification

**Version:** 1.0
**Last Updated:** 2026-03-05
**Owner:** Founder
**Audience:** Founder / Sales

---

## 1. Purpose
Decide fast if a lead is worth your time.

## 2. Who Does This
Founder or sales owner.

## 3. Tools You Need
- Website Reality Check template
- CRM (VSP-lite)
- Email

## 4. Starting Requirements
Before you start, make sure:
- [ ] You have the company name and website URL
- [ ] You know the city and industry

## 5. Step-by-Step Instructions

### Step 1: Run the quick website scan
1. Open the website on a phone.
2. Check if it looks modern.
3. Run a Lighthouse test.

**What you should see:** A clear list of issues.

### Step 2: Check ownership risk
1. Ask who owns the domain.
2. Ask who has access to hosting.

**Warning:** If they do not know, note “ownership risk.”

### Step 3: Decide fit
1. Mark the lead as “Fit” if they care about quality.
2. Mark the lead as “Not Fit” if they want cheapest.

**What you should see:** Lead stage updated in CRM.

## 6. Quality Check
After finishing, verify:
- [ ] Lead has a stage
- [ ] Next action is set
- [ ] Reality Check is attached

## 7. Common Problems and Fixes

| Problem | Why It Happens | How to Fix |
|---------|---------------|------------|
| Lead is vague | They do not know what they need | Offer the Reality Check call |
| They only want cheap | Price shopping | End fast and move on |

## 8. Notes
**Assumptions made:**
- This is a bootstrapped business. Time matters.
