# Local Expansion Roadmap — Gulf Coast Only

**Document Type:** Strategic Planning  
**Version:** 1.0  
**Date:** 2026-03-15  
**Strategy:** Option A — Gulf Coast Leader

---

## Overview

This roadmap defines Vantus Systems' geographic expansion strategy within the Gulf Coast region. We explicitly reject national expansion in favor of becoming the dominant regional provider before exploring adjacent markets.

**Exit Strategy:** Sale to regional MSP consolidator (All Covered, Thrive, or PE-backed buyer) for $8-12M in Year 3-5.

---

## Expansion Phases

### Phase 1: Foundation (Months 0-12)

**Focus:** Ft. Walton Beach & Immediate Surrounding Area

| Metric         | Target                              |
| -------------- | ----------------------------------- |
| Active Clients | 20                                  |
| Revenue        | $500K                               |
| Care Contracts | 0-5                                 |
| Primary Market | Ft. Walton Beach, Niceville, Destin |

**Activities:**

- Establish dominant position in immediate service area
- Build referral network with local business organizations
- Develop case studies and proof artifacts
- Test Care offering with early adopters

**Guardrails:**

- Do not actively pursue clients outside 30-mile radius
- Reject projects that don't fit target ICP (5-50 employees, outdated sites)

---

### Phase 2: Regional Expansion (Months 12-24)

**Focus:** Pensacola, FL + Mobile, AL

| Metric          | Target                   |
| --------------- | ------------------------ |
| Active Clients  | 40 additional (60 total) |
| Project Revenue | $1.16M                   |
| Care Contracts  | 10                       |
| Care ARR        | $240K                    |
| Total Revenue   | $1.4M                    |

**Activities:**

- Open secondary market presence in Pensacola
- Develop Mobile, AL market through partnerships
- Scale delivery team to support 40+ projects/year
- Establish Care as recognized recurring revenue stream

**Market Entry Strategy:**

- Pensacola: Partner with local business associations, target medical and legal practices
- Mobile: Leverage existing network, focus on B2B services and maritime industry

**Guardrails:**

- No more than 2-hour drive for in-person meetings
- Reject enterprise-class opportunities outside core ICP

---

### Phase 3: Market Dominance (Months 24-36)

**Focus:** Full Gulf Coast Coverage + Care Scale

| Metric              | Target                                                      |
| ------------------- | ----------------------------------------------------------- |
| Active Clients      | 50 total projects + 30 Care                                 |
| Project Revenue     | $1.5M                                                       |
| Care ARR            | $720K                                                       |
| Total Revenue       | $2.2M                                                       |
| Geographic Coverage | Ft. Walton, Pensacola, Mobile, Destin, Niceville, Crestview |

**Activities:**

- Complete coverage of Gulf Coast target markets
- Scale Care to 30+ contracts
- Build team: 2-3 delivery leads, 1 vCIO, support staff
- Prepare for exit conversations with regional MSP consolidators

**Exit Readiness:**

- Consistent 50%+ gross margins
- Documented SOPs for all delivery processes
- Proof artifacts for 20+ projects
- Recurring revenue (Care) at 30%+ of total

## Exit Planning

### Target Buyers

1. **All Covered** — National MSP consolidator, active in Southeast
2. **Thrive Networks** — Regional focus, Gulf Coast presence
3. **PE-backed regional consolidators** — Looking for profitable SMB-focused MSPs

### Exit Criteria

- $2M+ revenue with 30%+ Care mix
- 50%+ gross margins maintained
- 3+ years of documented profitability
- Strong local reputation and referral base

### Target Exit Value

- **Range:** $8-12M (4-5x revenue, 8-12x EBITDA)
- **Timing:** Year 3-5 (after establishing Care recurring revenue)
