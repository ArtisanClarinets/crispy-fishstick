## 1) Executive summary (5 bullets)

- Vantus wins locally by being the only shop that reliably delivers **enterprise-grade web + backend + ownership** for SMBs.
- The constraint is not skill. It’s **repeatability + distribution**.
- The first compounding engine is a **productized website + CMS package** that produces case studies and referrals.
- The second engine is a reusable internal platform (VSP) that reduces delivery time and increases quality consistency.
- Industry leadership comes from **proof artifacts + standardization**, not grand claims.

---

## 2) The problem (market reality)

The Gulf Coast SMB market is bifurcated:

- Low-end providers ship outdated sites with no backend or CMS.
- High-end agencies are expensive and often bundle retainers that don’t map cleanly to outcomes.

Most SMBs are stuck with:

- slow sites,
- no ability to update content,
- broken SEO,
- no clarity on who owns accounts/domains,
- and operational tools duct-taped together.

---

## 3) Our solution (what we sell)

**Enterprise-grade for small business**:

- Modern bespoke website with full content, SEO, and accessibility.
- CMS that is actually usable.
- Optional portals/admin modules when value justifies it.
- Deployment that fits reality: cloud, local, or hybrid.
- Owner handoff: credentials + documentation + runbooks.

We sell outcomes:

- credibility,
- leads,
- faster operations,
- reduced risk.

---

## 4) ICP and buyer journey

**ICP (first 90 days):**

- 5–50 employee SMBs with visible outdated sites.
- High-value leads (each lead matters).
- Decision maker is typically the owner.

**Buying journey:**

1. They feel embarrassed or “behind.”
2. They search locally and compare portfolios.
3. They fear getting ripped off or trapped.
4. They choose the vendor who feels safest and most credible.

Your edge is reducing fear with **proof + clarity**.

---

## 5) Differentiation and moat

**Differentiators (must be visible):**

- proof artifacts (Lighthouse, accessibility, security headers),
- CMS + backend capability,
- ownership/exit readiness,
- plain language deliverables.

**Moat:**

- internal standardization (Vantus Seed + VSP),
- SOP-driven delivery,
- compounding reputation + local trust.

## 6) 12–24 month strategic sequencing

- First 6 months: win via productized website package + referrals.
- Months 6–12: convert learnings into VSP modules that cut delivery time.

Details in `ROADMAP_12_24_MONTHS.md`.

---

## 7) Risks & mitigations

1. **Scope creep kills margin and time.**
   - Mitigation: strict scoping + change control SOP, modular add-ons, “content required” gate.
2. **You become a custom one-off shop.**
   - Mitigation: Vantus Seed baseline + VSP; every feature becomes a reusable module.
3. **You overbuild the platform before sales.**
   - Mitigation: VSP-lite first (pipeline + project tracking + checklist automation) before full portal.
