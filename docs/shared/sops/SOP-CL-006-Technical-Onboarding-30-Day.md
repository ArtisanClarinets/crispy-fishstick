# SOP-CL-006: Technical Onboarding (30-day)

## 1. PURPOSE
To provide a standardized 30-day technical onboarding process that ensures service readiness, secure configuration, and a smooth handoff to ongoing service delivery.

## 2. SCOPE
Applies to Onboarding Engineers, Delivery Managers, and Client IT contacts.

## 3. RESPONSIBILITIES
| Role | Responsibility |
|------|----------------|
| Onboarding Engineer | Execute technical tasks and document configurations |
| Delivery Manager | Oversee schedule and client communications |
| Client IT | Provide access and approvals |

## 4. PROCEDURE
### Week 0: Pre-Onboarding
- Verify access, gather credentials, schedule kickoff.

### Week 1: Inventory & Baseline
- Conduct inventory, deploy monitoring agents, baseline backups.

### Week 2: Hardening & Config
- Apply security hardening, patch systems, configure email/DNS protections.

### Week 3: Validation
- Test backups, run recovery drill for one sample workload, verify monitoring alerts.

### Week 4: Handoff
- Conduct training session, deliver knowledge transfer, schedule QBR.

## 5. TRIGGERS
- Contract Onboarding complete and scheduled start date arrived.

## 6. ESCALATION
- Any critical security findings escalate to CTO and Security Lead immediately.

## 7. QUALITY CRITERIA
- Onboarding completed within 30 days; key milestones signed off by client.
