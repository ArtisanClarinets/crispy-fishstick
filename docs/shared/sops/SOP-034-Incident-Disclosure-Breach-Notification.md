
# INCIDENT DISCLOSURE & BREACH NOTIFICATION

## I. PURPOSE & SCOPE

This SOP defines the procedures for disclosing security incidents and notifying affected parties in the event of a data breach. It applies to all security incidents that involve sensitive data.

## II. KEY PRINCIPLES

- **Timeliness:** Notify affected parties as soon as possible after a breach is discovered.
- **Transparency:** Be open and honest about what happened and what we are doing to fix it.
- **Compliance:** Comply with all legal and regulatory requirements for breach notification.
- **Empathy:** Communicate with empathy and respect for the affected parties.

## III. NOTIFICATION REQUIREMENTS

- **Legal and Regulatory:** We will comply with all applicable breach notification laws, such as the GDPR and HIPAA.
- **Contractual:** We will comply with all contractual requirements for breach notification.
- **Client and User:** We will notify our clients and users of any breach that affects their data.

## IV. STEP-BY-STEP PROCEDURE

1.  **Confirm the Breach:** Confirm that a data breach has occurred and determine the scope of the breach.
2.  **Activate the Incident Response Team:** Activate the incident response team to manage the breach.
3.  **Notify Legal Counsel:** Notify our legal counsel to ensure that we are complying with all legal requirements.
4.  **Draft the Notification:** Draft a breach notification that is clear, concise, and empathetic.
5.  **Approve the Notification:** The CISO and CEO must approve the breach notification before it is sent.
6.  **Send the Notification:** Send the breach notification to all affected parties.
7.  **Provide Support:** Provide support to the affected parties, such as credit monitoring services.
8.  **Conduct a Post-Mortem:** Conduct a post-mortem to identify the root cause of the breach and improve our security posture.

## V. TEMPLATES & CHECKLISTS

- **Breach Notification Template:** A template for drafting a breach notification.
- **Breach Notification Checklist:** A checklist of items to include in a breach notification.

## VI. ESCALATION PATHS

- All data breaches must be immediately reported to the CISO and the CEO.
- The decision to notify affected parties of a data breach will be made by the CEO, in consultation with the CISO and legal counsel.
