
# ON-CALL ROTATION & ESCALATION

## I. PURPOSE & SCOPE

This SOP defines the on-call rotation schedule, responsibilities, and escalation procedures to ensure 24/7 coverage for critical systems. It applies to all personnel in engineering and operations roles.

## II. KEY PRINCIPLES

- **Fairness:** On-call duties are distributed equitably among qualified team members.
- **Clarity:** On-call responsibilities and escalation paths are clearly documented and accessible.
- **Timeliness:** On-call engineers must acknowledge alerts within 15 minutes.
- **Sustainability:** The on-call schedule is designed to be sustainable and prevent burnout.

## III. ON-CALL ROTATION SCHEDULE

- **Rotation:** The on-call rotation is weekly, starting on Mondays at 10:00 AM local time.
- **Primary On-Call:** The primary on-call engineer is the first point of contact for all alerts.
- **Secondary On-Call:** The secondary on-call engineer is the backup if the primary is unavailable.
- **Handoff:** The outgoing on-call engineer must provide a written handoff summary to the incoming engineer.

## IV. STEP-BY-STEP PROCEDURE

1.  **Acknowledge Alert:** The primary on-call engineer must acknowledge alerts within 15 minutes.
2.  **Assess Impact:** Assess the impact of the alert and classify its severity.
3.  **Resolve or Escalate:** If the issue can be resolved quickly, the on-call engineer should do so. Otherwise, escalate according to the issue's severity.
4.  **Document Actions:** All actions taken must be documented in the incident ticket.
5.  **Post-Incident Review:** For Severity 1 and 2 incidents, a post-incident review must be conducted.

## V. TEMPLATES & CHECKLISTS

- **On-Call Handoff Template:** Use the standard on-call handoff template.
- **Incident Ticket Template:** Use the standard incident ticket template.

## VI. ESCALATION PATHS

| Severity | First Responder | Escalation 1 (after 15 mins) | Escalation 2 (after 30 mins) |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | Primary On-Call | Secondary On-Call | VP of Operations |
| 2 | Primary On-Call | Secondary On-Call | Engineering Manager |
| 3 | Primary On-Call | N/A | N/A |
| 4 | Primary On-Call | N/A | N/A |
