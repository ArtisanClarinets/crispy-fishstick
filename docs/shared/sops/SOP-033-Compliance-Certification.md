## I. PURPOSE & SCOPE

This SOP defines the procedures for achieving and maintaining compliance with relevant security and privacy regulations and certifications. It applies to all systems that handle sensitive data.

## II. KEY PRINCIPLES

- **Continuous Compliance:** Compliance is an ongoing process, not a one-time event.
- **Evidence-Based:** All compliance claims must be supported by evidence.
- **Automation:** Automate compliance checks wherever possible.
- **Transparency:** Be transparent with our clients about our compliance posture.

## III. COMPLIANCE FRAMEWORKS

Vantus Systems is committed to complying with the following frameworks:

- **SOC 2 Type II:** For security, availability, processing integrity, confidentiality, and privacy.
- **HIPAA:** For protecting the privacy and security of protected health information (PHI).
- **GDPR:** For protecting the data and privacy of individuals in the European Union.

## IV. STEP-BY-STEP PROCEDURE

1.  **Identify Compliance Requirements:** Identify the compliance requirements for each new system or service.
2.  **Implement Controls:** Implement the necessary controls to meet the compliance requirements.
3.  **Collect Evidence:** Collect evidence that the controls are in place and operating effectively.
4.  **Conduct Internal Audits:** Conduct internal audits to assess our compliance posture.
5.  **Conduct External Audits:** Engage an independent third-party auditor to conduct an external audit.
6.  **Remediate Findings:** Remediate any findings from the audits.
7.  **Maintain Compliance:** Continuously monitor our compliance posture and make improvements as needed.

## V. TEMPLATES & CHECKLISTS

- **Compliance Checklist:** A checklist of compliance requirements for each framework.
- **Evidence Collection Template:** A template for collecting evidence for compliance audits.

## VI. ESCALATION PATHS

- Any compliance gaps or issues must be reported to the CISO.
- If a compliance issue is not addressed in a timely manner, it will be escalated to the CEO and the board of directors.
