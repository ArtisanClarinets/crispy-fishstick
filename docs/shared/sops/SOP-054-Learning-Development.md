## I. PURPOSE & SCOPE

This SOP defines the process for learning and development at Vantus Systems. It aims to provide employees with opportunities to grow their skills and knowledge, and to support their career development.

## II. KEY PRINCIPLES

- **Continuous Learning:** We are committed to creating a culture of continuous learning.
- **Personalized Learning:** Learning and development opportunities should be tailored to the individual needs of each employee.
- **Alignment with Business Goals:** Learning and development should be aligned with the company's business goals.
- **Shared Responsibility:** Learning and development is a shared responsibility between the employee, their manager, and the company.

## III. LEARNING & DEVELOPMENT PROGRAMS

- **Tuition Reimbursement:** We offer tuition reimbursement for employees who are pursuing a degree or certification.
- **Conferences and Training:** We provide a budget for employees to attend conferences and training courses.
- **Internal Training:** We offer a variety of internal training programs.
- **Mentorship Program:** We have a mentorship program that pairs employees with experienced mentors.
- **Lunch and Learns:** We host regular lunch and learns on a variety of topics.

## IV. STEP-BY-STEP PROCEDURE

1.  **Create a Development Plan:** The employee and their manager work together to create a development plan.
2.  **Request Training:** The employee can request to attend a training course or conference.
3.  **Approve Request:** The employee's manager must approve the request.
4.  **Attend Training:** The employee attends the training.
5.  **Share Knowledge:** The employee shares what they have learned with their team.
6.  **Update Development Plan:** The employee and their manager update the development plan.

## V. TEMPLATES & CHECKLISTS

- **Development Plan Template:** A template for creating a development plan.
- **Training Request Form:** A form for requesting to attend a training course or conference.

## VI. ESCALATION PATHS

- If a training request is denied, the employee can escalate it to their manager's manager.
- If the issue cannot be resolved, it can be escalated to the VP of People.
