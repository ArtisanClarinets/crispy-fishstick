- ✅ Monthly access audits and manager confirmation
- ✅ Incident response for credential leaks
- ✅ Off-boarding procedures (immediate revocation)
- ✅ Templates: Access Request Form, Monthly Audit Report, Credential Rotation Checklist
- ✅ Decision trees and escalation paths
- ✅ Real-world examples (Tier 3 access, credential compromise, off-boarding)

**Impact:** Enterprise-grade zero-trust security. Audit trail for every access decision. No credential sprawl.

- ✅ Real-world examples (backend engineer onboarding, product manager onboarding)

**Impact:** New hires productive faster. Better retention. Consistent onboarding experience.
