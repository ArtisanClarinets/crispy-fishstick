# SOP-CL-001: Lead Qualification

## 1. PURPOSE
To define a consistent process for qualifying inbound and outbound leads to determine sales readiness and fit for Vantus services.

## 2. SCOPE
Applies to Sales Development Representatives (SDR), Account Executives (AE), and Onboarding teams during pre-sales and early engagement.

## 3. RESPONSIBILITIES
| Role | Responsibility |
|------|----------------|
| SDR | Initial contact, capture qualification data |
| AE | Validate fit, schedule discovery, handoff to technical assessment |
| VP Sales | Approve exceptions for custom pricing |

## 4. PROCEDURE
### Step 1: Initial Contact
- SDR captures company name, number of users, critical systems, and current pain points.
- Use the qualification form: [Lead Qualification Form Template]

### Step 2: Basic Qualification Criteria
- Company size: 1-500 users (no minimum required)
- Industry: Note regulated industries (requires Enterprise tier consideration)
- Budget: Indicate if budget is known or unknown
- Timeframe: Immediate (0-30 days), short (30-90 days), or long (>90 days)

### Step 3: Qualification Outcome
- If qualified, schedule Sales Discovery (SOP-CL-002).
- If not qualified (out of scope), mark as Nurture and add to CRM nurture sequence.

## 5. TRIGGERS
- New inbound lead via website, referral, or marketing campaign.

## 6. ESCALATION
- If legal/regulatory requirement identified (e.g., CUI, HIPAA), escalate to VP Sales & CTO for immediate review.

## 7. QUALITY CRITERIA
- Lead converted to discovery within 3 business days for qualified leads.
- Data completeness > 90% on qualification forms.
