
# OFF-BOARDING & EXIT INTERVIEWS

## I. PURPOSE & SCOPE

This SOP defines the process for off-boarding employees and conducting exit interviews. It aims to ensure a smooth and professional departure for all employees, and to gather feedback to improve the company.

## II. KEY PRINCIPLES

- **Professionalism:** The off-boarding process should be handled professionally and with respect for the departing employee.
- **Security:** All company assets must be returned and access to all systems must be revoked.
- **Knowledge Transfer:** All critical knowledge must be transferred from the departing employee to their replacement.
- **Feedback:** The exit interview is an opportunity to gather honest feedback to improve the company.

## III. OFF-BOARDING PROCESS

- **Resignation:** The employee submits their resignation to their manager.
- **Knowledge Transfer Plan:** The manager and employee create a knowledge transfer plan.
- **Asset Return:** The employee returns all company assets on their last day.
- **Access Revocation:** All access to company systems is revoked on the employee's last day.
- **Final Paycheck:** The employee receives their final paycheck in accordance with state law.

## IV. STEP-BY-STEP PROCEDURE

1.  **Receive Resignation:** The manager receives the employee's resignation.
2.  **Notify HR:** The manager notifies HR of the employee's resignation.
3.  **Create a Knowledge Transfer Plan:** The manager and employee create a knowledge transfer plan.
4.  **Schedule an Exit Interview:** HR schedules an exit interview with the employee.
5.  **Conduct the Exit Interview:** HR conducts the exit interview.
6.  **Return Assets:** The employee returns all company assets on their last day.
7.  **Revoke Access:** IT revokes all access to company systems.
8.  **Process Final Paycheck:** HR processes the employee's final paycheck.

## V. TEMPLATES & CHECKLISTS

- **Off-boarding Checklist:** A checklist of all the steps in the off-boarding process.
- **Exit Interview Form:** A form for conducting the exit interview.

## VI. ESCALATION PATHS

- If an employee fails to return company assets, it will be escalated to legal counsel.
- If an employee raises serious concerns during the exit interview, it will be escalated to the VP of People.
