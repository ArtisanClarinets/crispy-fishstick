## I. PURPOSE & SCOPE

This SOP defines the standards for all client communication to ensure consistency, professionalism, and clarity. It applies to all employees who interact with clients.

## II. KEY PRINCIPLES

- **Clarity:** Communication must be clear, concise, and unambiguous.
- **Timeliness:** Respond to client inquiries within 24 hours.
- **Professionalism:** Maintain a professional and respectful tone at all times.
- **Transparency:** Be open and honest in all client interactions.

## III. COMMUNICATION CHANNELS

- **Email:** For formal communication, project updates, and meeting summaries.
- **Slack:** For informal, real-time communication and quick questions.
- **Phone/Video Calls:** For scheduled meetings, complex discussions, and urgent issues.

## IV. STEP-BY-STEP PROCEDURE

1.  **Acknowledge Receipt:** Acknowledge receipt of all client inquiries within 4 business hours.
2.  **Provide a Timeline:** If an immediate answer is not possible, provide a timeline for a full response.
3.  **Document Communication:** Log all significant client communication in the project's communication log.
4.  **Meeting Etiquette:** For all meetings, send an agenda beforehand and a summary with action items afterward.

## V. TEMPLATES & CHECKLISTS

- **Email Signature:** All employees must use the standard company email signature.
- **Meeting Agenda Template:** Use the standard meeting agenda template for all client meetings.
- **Meeting Summary Template:** Use the standard meeting summary template for all client meetings.

## VI. ESCALATION PATHS

- If a client issue cannot be resolved, escalate it to the Project Manager.
- If the Project Manager cannot resolve the issue, escalate it to the VP of Delivery.
