# SOP-CL-004: Proposal Development

## 1. PURPOSE
To define the process for converting a qualified opportunity and technical assessment into a formal proposal and SOW.

## 2. SCOPE
Applies to Account Executives, Proposal Authors, and Legal for contract review.

## 3. RESPONSIBILITIES
| Role | Responsibility |
|------|----------------|
| AE | Lead proposal creation and pricing selection |
| Proposal Author | Draft SOW, deliverables, timelines |
| Legal | Review contract terms and verify compliance clauses |

## 4. PROCEDURE
### Step 1: Choose Pricing Tier
- Based on Technical Assessment and client needs, select Foundation/Advanced/Premier and any minimal add-ons.

### Step 2: Draft SOW
- Document scope, deliverables, timelines, acceptance criteria, and payment terms.

### Step 3: Internal Review
- Proposal reviewed by VP Sales and Solutions Architect for technical and commercial acceptability.

### Step 4: Client Presentation
- Present proposal and gather feedback. If accepted, initiate Contract Onboarding (SOP-CL-005).

## 5. TRIGGERS
- Completion of Technical Assessment (SOP-CL-003) and client interest to proceed.

## 6. ESCALATION
- For deviations from published pricing greater than 10%, escalate to CFO/CEO for approval.

## 7. QUALITY CRITERIA
- Proposal accuracy verified; pricing matches published table unless discount approved.
