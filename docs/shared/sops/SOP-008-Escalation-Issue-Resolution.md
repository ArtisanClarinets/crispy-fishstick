## I. PURPOSE & SCOPE

This SOP defines the process for escalating and resolving client issues. It applies to all employees and contractors.

## II. KEY PRINCIPLES

- **Ownership:** Every issue must have a clear owner responsible for its resolution.
- **Timeliness:** Issues must be resolved within the agreed-upon SLA.
- **Communication:** Keep all stakeholders informed of the issue's status.
- **Root Cause Analysis:** For every significant issue, perform a root cause analysis to prevent recurrence.

## III. ISSUE SEVERITY LEVELS

- **Severity 1 (Critical):** System down, major business impact.
- **Severity 2 (High):** Significant functionality impaired, major business impact.
- **Severity 3 (Medium):** Minor functionality impaired, moderate business impact.
- **Severity 4 (Low):** Cosmetic issue, minimal business impact.

## IV. STEP-BY-STEP PROCEDURE

1.  **Log the Issue:** Log all client issues in the issue tracking system.
2.  **Assign Severity:** Assign a severity level to the issue.
3.  **Assign Owner:** Assign an owner to the issue.
4.  **Resolve the Issue:** The owner is responsible for resolving the issue within the SLA.
5.  **Perform Root Cause Analysis:** For Severity 1 and 2 issues, perform a root cause analysis and document the findings.
6.  **Communicate Resolution:** Communicate the resolution to the client and all stakeholders.

## V. TEMPLATES & CHECKLISTS

- **Issue Report Template:** Use the standard issue report template for all new issues.
- **Root Cause Analysis Template:** Use the standard root cause analysis template for all Severity 1 and 2 issues.
