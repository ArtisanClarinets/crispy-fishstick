## I. PURPOSE & SCOPE

This SOP defines the process for reviewing and adjusting employee compensation and benefits. It aims to provide a fair and competitive compensation and benefits package to all employees.

## II. KEY PRINCIPLES

- **Fairness:** Compensation should be fair and equitable.
- **Competitiveness:** Our compensation and benefits package should be competitive with the market.
- **Transparency:** The process for determining compensation should be transparent.
- **Performance-Based:** Compensation should be tied to performance.

## III. COMPENSATION REVIEW PROCESS

- **Annual Review:** We conduct a comprehensive review of our compensation and benefits package once a year.
- **Market Data:** We use market data to ensure that our compensation is competitive.
- **Performance Reviews:** Performance reviews are a key input into the compensation review process.
- **Salary Bands:** We have salary bands for all of our major roles.

## IV. STEP-BY-STEP PROCEDURE

1.  **Gather Market Data:** Gather market data on compensation and benefits.
2.  **Review Salary Bands:** Review and update the salary bands.
3.  **Conduct Performance Reviews:** Conduct performance reviews.
4.  **Make Compensation Recommendations:** Managers make compensation recommendations for their employees.
5.  **Approve Recommendations:** The VP of People and the CEO approve the compensation recommendations.
6.  **Communicate Changes:** Communicate any changes to compensation to employees.

## V. TEMPLATES & CHECKLISTS

- **Compensation Review Checklist:** A checklist of all the steps in the compensation review process.
- **Salary Band Documents:** Documents that define the salary bands for each role.

## VI. ESCALATION PATHS

- If an employee has a question about their compensation, they can ask their manager.
- If the manager cannot answer the question, it can be escalated to the VP of People.
