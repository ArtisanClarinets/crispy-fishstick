# SOP-CL-005: Contract Onboarding

## 1. PURPOSE
To ensure a smooth and compliant transition from signed agreement to service commencement.

## 2. SCOPE
Applies to Legal, Billing, Onboarding, and Delivery teams.

## 3. RESPONSIBILITIES
| Role | Responsibility |
|------|----------------|
| Legal | Execute contract and record terms |
| Billing | Set up billing schedule and payment method |
| Onboarding Lead | Create onboarding plan and assign delivery team |

## 4. PROCEDURE
### Step 1: Record Contract
- Store signed contract in CRM and document key terms (start date, billing, SLA tier).

### Step 2: Billing Setup
- Verify payment method and first invoice amount.

### Step 3: Onboarding Kickoff
- Schedule kickoff meeting with client stakeholders and share 30-day technical onboarding plan.

## 5. TRIGGERS
- Signed contract and receipt of first payment authorization.

## 6. ESCALATION
- If contract terms conflict with published pricing, escalate to Legal & VP Sales.

## 7. QUALITY CRITERIA
- Billing set up within 5 business days; kickoff scheduled within 3 business days of start date.
