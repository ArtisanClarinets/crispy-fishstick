# SOP-CL-002: Sales Discovery

## 1. PURPOSE
To perform a structured sales discovery that captures technical, business, and contractual requirements to prepare a proposal.

## 2. SCOPE
Applies to Account Executives (AE), Sales Engineers (SE), and Solutions Architects during the discovery phase.

## 3. RESPONSIBILITIES
| Role | Responsibility |
|------|----------------|
| AE | Lead discovery, collect business requirements |
| SE | Conduct technical discovery and system inventory |
| Solutions Architect | Validate architecture constraints and propose solution overview |

## 4. PROCEDURE
### Step 1: Schedule Discovery Call
- Schedule with stakeholders and gather attendee list and objectives.

### Step 2: Business Requirements
- Capture business goals, top pain points, uptime requirements, compliance needs, and decision timeline.

### Step 3: Technical Assessment (High-level)
- Number of users, servers, critical applications, cloud dependencies, remote sites, network topology summary.

### Step 4: Initial Recommendation
- Recommend tier (Foundation/Advanced/Premier) based on requirements, identify needed add-ons.

### Step 5: Handoff
- Prepare a discovery summary document and handoff to Proposal Development (SOP-CL-004).

## 5. TRIGGERS
- Qualified lead from SOP-CL-001 or inbound request for discovery.

## 6. ESCALATION
- If the prospect requires immediate compliance work, escalate to CTO and VP Sales for pricing exceptions.

## 7. QUALITY CRITERIA
- Discovery summary completed within 3 business days of the call.
