# SOP-CL-010: Offboarding

## 1. PURPOSE
To ensure a controlled and documented offboarding process that preserves data portability, protects customer data, and minimizes operational risk.

## 2. SCOPE
Applies to Onboarding/Offboarding Engineers, Delivery Managers, Legal, and Billing.

## 3. RESPONSIBILITIES
| Role | Responsibility |
|------|----------------|
| Onboarding/Offboarding Engineer | Execute data exports and transfer tasks |
| Legal | Review contract terms regarding data retention |
| Billing | Finalize final invoices and close accounts |

## 4. PROCEDURE
### Step 1: Offboarding Request Received
- Verify request authenticity and confirm scope (full offboard, partial, migration to new provider).

### Step 2: Data Export & Handover
- Provide agreed exports (backups, configuration docs), transfer ownership of accounts as required.

### Step 3: Service Termination
- Deprovision monitoring agents, close backups after confirmation, and remove access per retention policy.

### Step 4: Finalization
- Provide exit report and checklist to client, schedule follow-up to ensure no residual issues.

## 5. TRIGGERS
- Contract termination, client request, or failure to renew per SOP-CL-009.

## 6. ESCALATION
- If client data breach is suspected during offboarding, escalate to Security Lead and Legal immediately.

## 7. QUALITY CRITERIA
- All data exports delivered within SLA and validated by client; no access remains after confirmed termination.
