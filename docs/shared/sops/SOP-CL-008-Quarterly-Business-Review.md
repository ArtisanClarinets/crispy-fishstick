# SOP-CL-008: Quarterly Business Review (QBR)

## 1. PURPOSE
To conduct a structured Quarterly Business Review with clients to review performance, roadmap, and business outcomes.

## 2. SCOPE
Applies to Account Managers, vCIOs, and Delivery Managers for all active managed service clients.

## 3. RESPONSIBILITIES
| Role | Responsibility |
|------|----------------|
| vCIO | Lead QBR, present roadmap and risk analysis |
| Account Manager | Coordinate meeting & capture action items |
| Delivery Manager | Present operational metrics and SLA performance |

## 4. PROCEDURE
### Pre-QBR
- Gather KPIs: SLA adherence, incident summary, security posture, uptime metrics, and recommended actions.

### QBR Meeting
- Present executive dashboard, review roadmap, agree on priorities and POAM.

### Post-QBR
- Publish minutes, assign action items, and schedule follow-up.

## 5. TRIGGERS
- Every 90 days from contract start or as requested by client.

## 6. ESCALATION
- Unresolved high-risk items escalate to client executive sponsor and Vantus executive team.

## 7. QUALITY CRITERIA
- QBR delivered on schedule; action items closed or on-track at next QBR.
