# SOP-CL-009: Renewal Management

## 1. PURPOSE
To manage contract renewals proactively and maximize retention while honoring transparent pricing commitments.

## 2. SCOPE
Applies to Account Managers, Billing, and Legal for all clients with expiring agreements.

## 3. RESPONSIBILITIES
| Role | Responsibility |
|------|----------------|
| Account Manager | Engage client 90/60/30 days before renewal |
| Billing | Prepare renewal invoices and payment options |
| Legal | Review any contract amendments |

## 4. PROCEDURE
### Timeline
- 90 days: Account Manager begins renewal conversation and reviews service value
- 60 days: Present renewal proposal and any adjustments
- 30 days: Finalize renewal terms and collect signatures

### Pricing Considerations
- Standard: published pricing applies
- Any discount must be pre-approved per Pricing Policy

## 5. TRIGGERS
- Contract expiration date within 90 days.

## 6. ESCALATION
- If client disputes pricing or service levels, escalate to VP Sales and COO.

## 7. QUALITY CRITERIA
- Renewals closed or have active plan >30 days before contract end.
