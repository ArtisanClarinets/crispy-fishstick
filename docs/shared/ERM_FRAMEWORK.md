# VS-GOV-501: Global Enterprise Risk Management (ERM) Framework

**Version:** 1.0.0  
**Effective Date:** March 3, 2026  
**Classification:** Internal — Enterprise Governance  
**Alignment:** ISO 31000:2018

---

## 1. Executive Summary

This framework establishes the Vantus Systems approach to identifying, assessing, and mitigating risks across the global enterprise. As we scale toward $500M in revenue, proactive risk management is essential for maintaining operational resilience and protecting stakeholder value.

## 2. Risk Governance Structure

| Tier | Role | Responsibility |
|------|------|----------------|
| **Tier 1** | Board Risk Committee | Strategic oversight and risk appetite setting |
| **Tier 2** | Executive Risk Council | Operational oversight and mitigation tracking |
| **Tier 3** | Risk Owners | Day-to-day management of specific risk categories |

## 3. Risk Appetite Statement

Vantus Systems maintains a **Low** risk appetite for security and compliance failures, a **Moderate** appetite for operational risks during rapid scale, and a **Strategic** appetite for innovation and market expansion.

## 4. Risk Categories

### 4.1 Strategic Risk
- Market disruption
- Brand reputation
- Revenue achievement ($500M Target)

### 4.2 Operational Risk
- Service delivery failure
- Infrastructure downtime
- Talent acquisition and retention

### 4.3 Financial Risk
- Capital availability
- Currency fluctuation (International Expansion)
- Credit risk

### 4.4 Compliance & Legal Risk
- Regulatory changes (GDPR, CMMC)
- Intellectual property protection
- Contractual liability

## 5. Assessment Methodology (ISO 31000)

### 5.1 Identification
Continuous scanning of the internal and external environment to detect potential threats.

### 5.2 Analysis (Likelihood x Impact)
- **Likelihood:** 1 (Rare) to 5 (Almost Certain)
- **Impact:** 1 (Negligible) to 5 (Catastrophic)

### 5.3 Evaluation
Prioritizing risks based on their Risk Score (Likelihood x Impact).

| Score | Category | Action |
|-------|----------|--------|
| 16-25 | Critical | Immediate mitigation required; Board notification |
| 10-15 | High | Mitigation plan within 30 days |
| 5-9 | Medium | Periodic monitoring |
| 1-4 | Low | Acceptance |

## 6. Treatment Strategies

1. **Avoid:** Eliminate the activity creating the risk.
2. **Mitigate:** Implement controls to reduce likelihood or impact.
3. **Transfer:** Use insurance or contractual clauses to shift risk.
4. **Accept:** Acknowledge the risk and monitor performance.

## 7. Reporting & Monitoring

- **Monthly Risk Dashboard:** Presented to the Executive Risk Council.
- **Quarterly ERM Report:** Presented to the Board Risk Committee.
- **Annual Risk Review:** Comprehensive audit of the framework and controls.
