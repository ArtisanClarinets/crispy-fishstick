## What This Catalog Is

## Our Service Philosophy

We focus on systems where:
- Failure is expensive (downtime costs money or reputation).
- Clarity is critical (you need to understand and control what you have).
- Longevity matters (this system needs to run for years, not months).

We do not do "experimental R&D" or "move fast and break things." We build systems that work, last, and can be handed off.
