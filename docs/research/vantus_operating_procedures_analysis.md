## EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

This document analyzes the operational maturity of 10 Gulf Coast MSP competitors, reverse-engineers their procedures from public signals, and establishes the SOP framework for Vantus Systems to achieve industry-leading operational excellence.

**Key Finding:** Digital Boardwalk represents the gold standard in operational maturity (custom AI, 800+ documented SOPs, SOC 2 compliance). Most competitors operate at medium or low maturity with significant gaps Vantus can exploit.

**Strategic Imperative:** Vantus must leapfrog the market by implementing documented, automated, and client-transparent operational procedures from day one.

**Market Reality:** Most MSPs wait for tickets.

**Vantus Differentiation:**
- Predictive maintenance
- Automated issue prevention
- Health checks before failures
- Capacity planning alerts

**Benefit:** Fewer incidents, higher satisfaction

**Market Reality:** Static procedures, no iteration.

**Vantus Differentiation:**
- Monthly SOP reviews

## 6. SLA RECOMMENDATIONS

### 6.1 Response Time SLAs

| Severity | Definition | Foundation | Professional | Enterprise |
|----------|------------|------------|--------------|------------|
| **P1 - Critical** | Business operations stopped; security breach | 2 hours | 30 minutes | 15 minutes |
| **P2 - High** | Major function impaired; multiple users affected | 4 hours | 2 hours | 1 hour |
| **P3 - Normal** | Minor issue; single user affected | 8 hours | 4 hours | 2 hours |
| **P4 - Low** | Question; enhancement request; non-urgent | 24 hours | 8 hours | 4 hours |

**Availability:**
- Foundation: Business hours (8am-8pm EST, M-F)
- Professional: Extended hours (7am-10pm EST, M-F)
- Enterprise: 24/7/365

### 6.2 Resolution Time SLAs

| Severity | Foundation | Professional | Enterprise |
|----------|------------|--------------|------------|
| P1 | 4 hours | 2 hours | 1 hour |
| P2 | 24 hours | 8 hours | 4 hours |
| P3 | 72 hours | 48 hours | 24 hours |
| P4 | 5 business days | 3 business days | 2 business days |

### 6.3 Uptime SLAs

| Metric | Foundation | Professional | Enterprise |
|--------|------------|--------------|------------|
| **Network uptime** | 99.5% | 99.9% | 99.95% |
| **Server availability** | 99.5% | 99.9% | 99.95% |
| **Application availability** | 99.0% | 99.5% | 99.9% |

**Downtime Calculations:**
- 99.5% = 3.65 hours/month max downtime
- 99.9% = 43.8 minutes/month max downtime
- 99.95% = 21.9 minutes/month max downtime

### 6.4 Service Credit Policy

If Vantus fails to meet SLAs:

| Missed SLA | Service Credit |
|------------|----------------|
| Response time | 5% of monthly fee |
| Resolution time | 10% of monthly fee |
| Uptime SLA | 25% of monthly fee |
| Multiple misses (3+ in month) | 50% of monthly fee |

**Credit Request:**
- Client must request within 30 days
- Credit applied to next invoice
- Maximum credit: 100% of monthly fee
