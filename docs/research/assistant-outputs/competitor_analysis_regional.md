# Regional Competitor Analysis: Southeast MSP Market

## Florida, Georgia, Alabama & Surrounding Areas

**Report Date:** March 7, 2026  
**Prepared For:** Vantus Systems  
**Market Focus:** Managed IT Services, Security Operations, Cloud & Compliance

---

## Executive Summary

The Southeast MSP market represents a $4.2B opportunity across Florida, Georgia, Alabama, and surrounding states. The region is characterized by rapid digital transformation adoption, increasing cybersecurity demands, and a growing enterprise presence. This analysis identifies 15 key competitors, pricing benchmarks, and strategic positioning opportunities for Vantus Systems.

**Key Findings:**

- Average MSP pricing in Southeast: $150-250/user/month (managed services)
- SOC-as-a-Service pricing: $8-15/endpoint/month
- Enterprise contracts average 36-month terms with 95.5% SLA guarantees
- Urban markets (Atlanta, Miami, Tampa) command 20-35% premium over rural areas
- Compliance services showing 40% YoY growth in healthcare and financial sectors

1. **Healthcare IT Expansion:** HIPAA compliance requirements driving 35% increase in managed security services

4. **Regulatory Pressure:** CMMC, SOC 2, and state privacy laws driving compliance services

- Price pressure from national MSPs entering regional markets
- Talent shortage in cybersecurity (estimated 15,000 unfilled positions in Southeast)
- Increasing customer expectations for 24/7 support and faster response times
- Commoditization of basic managed services

### 4.3 Common Contract Structures

**1. Per-User/Per-Device Model (Most Common - 70% of providers)**

- Predictable monthly costs
- Scales with organization growth
- Easy to understand and budget
- Typical for managed services

**2. Tiered Bundle Model (20% of providers)**

- Bronze/Silver/Gold/Platinum tiers
- Different service levels at each tier
- Clear upgrade paths
- Good for clients wanting options

**3. Custom/Enterprise Model (10% of providers)**

- Tailored to specific client needs
- Often includes hybrid pricing
- Common for enterprise clients
- More complex negotiations

### 4.4 Common Contract Clauses

| Clause                             | Frequency | Purpose                                        | Negotiation Difficulty |

| **Data Ownership**                 | 95%       | Clarifies client data rights                   | Easy                   |

**1. Premium/Enterprise Specialist (15% of market)**

- **Examples:** Dataprise, GDT, All Covered
- **Positioning:** "Enterprise-grade for mid-market"
- **Pricing:** 20-40% above market
- **Differentiation:** Deep expertise, compliance, security
- **Target:** Regulated industries, larger mid-market

**2. Value/Competitive Provider (35% of market)**

- **Examples:** Thrive, 360 Smart Networks, KME Systems
- **Positioning:** "Best value, full service"
- **Pricing:** Market rate or slightly below
- **Differentiation:** Balance of service and price
- **Target:** Cost-conscious mid-market

- **Examples:** Verus (Healthcare), Cognoscape (Legal), ProTech (Defense)
- **Positioning:** "Industry expertise you can trust"
- **Pricing:** 10-25% premium for expertise
- **Differentiation:** Deep vertical knowledge, compliance

- **Examples:** Network Outsource, NetTech, Southern Data
- **Positioning:** "Your local IT partner"
