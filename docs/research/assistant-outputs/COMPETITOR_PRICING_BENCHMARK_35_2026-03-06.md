## C) Market Band Synthesis (No-Care Offers)

- Website Rebuild market visibility spans low lead-gen anchors to premium execution.
- Website + CMS pricing materially increases when governance, migrations, and workflows are included.
- Portal/business system work shifts into high-complexity software delivery and should be anchored accordingly.
