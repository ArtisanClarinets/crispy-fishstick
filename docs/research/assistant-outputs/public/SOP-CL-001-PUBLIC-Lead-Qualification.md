# SOP-CL-001-PUBLIC: Lead Qualification — Public Summary

## Purpose
To define our lead qualification process and ensure every prospective client receives a timely, professional response.

## Our Commitment to You

### Response Time
- **Initial response within 24 hours** during business days
- **Qualified leads** receive a discovery scheduling within 3 business days

### What We Evaluate
- Company size and IT requirements
- Current infrastructure and pain points
- Compliance needs (if any)
- Budget and timeline

### Our Promise
We never share your information with third parties. Your data is protected under our Privacy Policy.

---

## How It Works

1. **You Reach Out** — Via website, phone, or referral
2. **We Respond** — Within 24 hours with initial questions
3. **We Qualify** — Determine if we're the right fit
4. **We Schedule** — Discovery call if qualified
