## 4. SALES PLAYBOOK

### 4.1 Value Proposition (30 Seconds)

> "Vantus is the only national MSP that publishes full, transparent pricing. While other IT providers hide fees and force you to 'contact us for a quote,' we show you exactly what you'll pay—online, instantly. We include on-site support, VoIP phones, and cloud optimization in our tiers, so when you compare the total cost, we're often 20-30% less than 'cheaper' options that nickel-and-dime you."

### 4.2 Common Objections

| Objection | Response |
|-----------|----------|
| "You're more expensive" | "Did that quote include on-site support? VoIP? Cloud optimization? Our price is all-inclusive—compare the total cost, not just the per-user number." |
| "I need to think about it" | "Absolutely. Can you tell me what you're comparing against? I'll help you do an apples-to-apples comparison." |
| "We already have an MSP" | "Great—that's the baseline. But are you happy with their pricing transparency? Do you know what you're paying for every service? Most clients find See pricing/pricing_public.yaml-500/mo in savings when we optimize their stack." |
| "Your price is too low—why?" | "We're not low—we're transparent. Others hide fees. We don't. Our price includes more, so it looks higher, but your total cost is often lower." |

### 4.3 Closing Techniques

1. **The Calculator Close**
   > "Can you pull up your current IT bill? Let's run it through our calculator and see exactly what you'd save with Vantus."

2. **The Guarantee Close**
   > "We're the only MSP offering a 90-day satisfaction guarantee. If you're not happy, full refund—no questions. What do you have to lose?"

3. **The Comparison Close**
   > "Request proposals from 2 other MSPs and bring them to our call. We'll help you understand what's really included in each."

### 4.4 Discovery Questions

1. "How many users and servers do you have today?"
2. "What's your current IT budget—monthly and annually?"
3. "What are your biggest pain points with your current provider?"
4. "Do you have any compliance requirements—HIPAA, SOC 2, CMMC?"
5. "What's your timeline for making a change?"
