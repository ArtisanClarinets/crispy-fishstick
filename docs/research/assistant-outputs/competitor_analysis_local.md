## EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

This report provides comprehensive competitor pricing and market positioning analysis for Vantus Systems in the Emerald Coast / Gulf Coast Florida market. The analysis covers 10 local/regional competitors and 5 national benchmarks across 10 service categories.

### Key Findings:

- **Market Gap:** No competitor offers unified IT + VoIP + Compliance with transparent pricing
- **Pricing Opportunity:** 40-60% of competitors hide pricing (opportunity for differentiation)
- **Local Weakness:** Most local providers lack compliance expertise (CMMC, HIPAA)
- **Price Range:** MSP services range $100-$300/user/month locally
