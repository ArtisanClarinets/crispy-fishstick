# RISK MITIGATION

## Identified Risks & Mitigation Strategies

### Risk 1: Competitor Aggressive Response
**Likelihood:** Medium | **Impact:** High

**Scenario:** Digital Boardwalk or Cortavo lowers prices or increases marketing in response to Vantus positioning.

**Mitigation:**
- Lead on value, not price
- Emphasize lock-in risks (competitors can't match this)
- Document competitive wins with proof points
- Maintain pricing discipline

**Trigger:** Competitor mentions Vantus by name in sales calls

---

### Risk 2: Market Confusion on "Owner-Controlled Infrastructure"
**Likelihood:** Medium | **Impact:** Medium

**Scenario:** Prospects don't understand what "owner-controlled" means or assume it's only for government/defense.

**Mitigation:**
- Create educational content explaining the concept
- Use clearer terms: "self-hosted options," "exit-ready," "vendor neutral"
- Lead with benefits, not terminology
- A/B test messaging for clarity

**Trigger:** More than 3 prospects ask for clarification in discovery calls

---

### Risk 3: CMMC Timeline Delays
**Likelihood:** Low | **Impact:** Medium

**Scenario:** CMMC 2.0 implementation is delayed beyond current timeline.

**Mitigation:**
- Build for current NIST 800-171 requirements (which CMMC is based on)
- Stay informed via CMMC-AB and DoD sources
- Position as "CMMC-ready" not "CMMC-certified"
- Maintain flexibility in compliance messaging

**Trigger:** Official DoD announcement of timeline changes

---

### Risk 4: Local Competitor Acquisitions
**Likelihood:** High | **Impact:** Low

**Scenario:** Regional MSPs are acquired by larger national players, increasing competition.

**Mitigation:**
- Monitor local M&A activity
- Position as independent, local alternative
- Emphasize relationship continuity risk in acquisitions
- Build referral network before acquisitions happen

**Trigger:** Announced acquisition of any Gulf Coast MSP

---

### Risk 5: Lead Quality from Server Planner
**Likelihood:** Medium | **Impact:** Medium

**Scenario:** Server Planner attracts tire-kickers with low purchase intent.

**Mitigation:**
- Add qualification questions in tool
- Score leads based on inputs (company size, timeline, budget)
- Create segmented nurture sequences
- Sales team trained on qualification

**Trigger:** Less than 20% of Server Planner leads book discovery calls
