## MARKET HEADLINE

The Gulf Coast MSP market is dominated by opaque pricing, Azure/M365 lock-in strategies, and unverifiable security claims—creating a clear opportunity for Vantus Systems to differentiate through transparent pricing, independent governance infrastructure options, and provable security baselines. Recent high-profile breaches in Alabama (May 2025) and Florida (July 2025) have heightened buyer awareness, while CMMC 2.0 compliance deadlines are creating urgency for defense contractors. Vantus's proof-first approach and vendor-neutral positioning address market gaps that competitors systematically ignore.
