## Why CPA Firms?

CPA firms represent an ideal initial vertical for Vantus Systems because they align perfectly with our core value proposition:

### Alignment with Vantus Strengths

| CPA Firm Need | Vantus Solution |
|---------------|-----------------|
| **Compliance requirements** (SOC 2, client data protection) | Security-first architecture, audit-ready documentation |
| **Audit/documentation culture** | Owner Handoff Package, Architecture Decision Records |
| **Recurring technology needs** | Vantus Care ongoing support model |
| **Vendor independence concerns** | Client Independence Roadmap, no lock-in guarantee |
| **Professional reputation risk** | Provable security (Lighthouse 95+, security headers) |

### Market Characteristics

- **Size:** 10-50 employee firms are underserved by enterprise MSPs
- **Budget:** $5K-50K project budgets, $1-3K monthly recurring feasible
- **Decision-maker:** Managing Partner or IT Director (accessible)
- **Pain points:** Tax season uptime, client portal security, data ownership
- **Geographic concentration:** Professional services clusters in metro areas

---

## Target Profile

### Ideal Firm Characteristics

- **Size:** 10-50 employees
- **Services:** Tax, audit, advisory (not just bookkeeping)
- **Technology:** Currently using cloud accounting software (QuickBooks Online, Xero, etc.)
- **Pain:** Has experienced downtime, data loss, or vendor lock-in issues
- **Awareness:** Concerned about client data security and compliance
- **Location:** Gulf Coast region (Pensacola, Mobile, Panama City, Tallahassee)

### Disqualifiers

- Sole practitioners (too small, not enough budget)
- Firms already locked into 3+ year MSP contracts
- Firms with internal IT staff (not our target)
- Firms using only desktop software (no cloud migration interest)

## CPA-Specific Messaging

### Value Propositions

1. **"Your Clients Trust You With Their Data—Can You Say the Same About Your IT Provider?"**
   - Emphasize audit trails, documentation, and provable security

2. **"What Happens During Tax Season If Your Systems Go Down?"**
   - Focus on uptime guarantees, disaster recovery, and business continuity

3. **"Who Owns Your Client Portal—You or Your SaaS Vendor?"**
   - Highlight vendor independence and data ownership

### CPA-Specific Audit Offering

**Name:** "CPA Firm Technology Independence Audit"

**Deliverable:**
- Client data flow mapping (where does client data live?)
- Vendor lock-in risk assessment
- Tax season readiness evaluation
- Compliance gap analysis (SOC 2 readiness)
- Exit strategy documentation

**Price:** $2,500 (or free with Build engagement)

**Timeline:** 5 business days

## Risks and Mitigations

| Risk | Mitigation |
|------|------------|
| Tax season timing (Jan-Apr) | Avoid major projects during tax season; focus on audits and planning |
| Compliance complexity | Partner with CPA-focused compliance consultants for specialized requirements |
| Seasonal cash flow | Structure payment terms around CPA firm cash flow (heavier post-tax season) |
| Competition from CPA-specific MSPs | Differentiate on vendor independence (most CPA MSPs push Microsoft/Azure) |
