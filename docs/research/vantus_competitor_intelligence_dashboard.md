**Primary Win Factors:**
1. **Pricing Transparency** (vs. 8 of 10 competitors)
2. **No Surcharges** (on-site included)
3. **Unified Platform** (IT + VoIP + Compliance)
4. **Local Presence** (vs. national competitors)
5. **No Minimums** (serve any size business)
6. **SOP Transparency** (documented procedures)
7. **Owner-Controlled Infrastructure** (vs. cloud lock-in)

**Secondary Win Factors:**
8. Faster response times (15-min SLA)
9. Compliance expertise (CMMC, HIPAA, SOC 2)
10. 90-day satisfaction guarantee
11. Exit assistance (no hostage data)
12. Vendor neutrality

### 3.2 Why Vantus Loses

**Primary Loss Factors:**
1. **Brand Recognition** (newer company)
2. **Review Volume** (vs. Digital Boardwalk's 850+)

- Publish SOPs publicly (build trust)
- Aggressive review solicitation program
- Case studies with measurable outcomes
- Partner with C3PAO for CMMC audits
- Highlight 90-day guarantee (risk reversal)

### 4.1 When Prospect Mentions Digital Boardwalk

**Script 1: On Pricing**
```
"Digital Boardwalk is a solid company with strong processes. One thing to

that's an additional $750-1,050 monthly on top of their base price. They 
also have a 100-user minimum for their published pricing.

Our pricing includes on-site support with no surcharges, and we have no 
minimum user requirements. Here's an apples-to-apples cost comparison for 
your environment..."
```

**Script 2: On Process Maturity**
```
"Digital Boardwalk talks about their 800+ documented SOPs. That's impressive, 
and something we respect. Here's how we're approaching operational excellence:
```
