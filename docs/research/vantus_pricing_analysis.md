## EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

Based on comprehensive competitive analysis of 10 Gulf Coast and national MSP competitors, Vantus Systems has a clear opportunity to win market share through aggressive pricing transparency, value-packed tiers, and elimination of industry-standard "gotchas" (on-site surcharges, hidden fees, minimum requirements).

**The Market Reality:**
- Only 2 of 10 competitors publish transparent pricing (Cortavo, Digital Boardwalk)
- Industry average: $125-$150/user for full managed services
- Most competitors surcharge for on-site support
- Hardware inclusion is rare (only Cortavo)
- Compliance services are premium add-ons

**Vantus Opportunity:** Aggressive transparency + value-stacking + elimination of surcharges = market disruption

## 2. PRICING MODEL ANALYSIS

### 2.1 Common Pricing Models in Market

**Model 1: Per-User (Most Common)**
- **Example:** Cortavo ($150-$205/user)
- **Pros:** Simple to understand, predictable
- **Cons:** Doesn't account for device-heavy environments
- **Best for:** Standard office environments, knowledge workers

**Model 2: Per-User + Per-Server (Growing)**
- **Example:** Digital Boardwalk ($100-$143/user + $250-$379/server)
- **Pros:** More accurate for server-heavy environments
- **Cons:** Complexity, server pricing often opaque
- **Best for:** Mixed environments, manufacturing, healthcare

**Model 3: Per-Device (Rare)**
- **Example:** VTC Tech hybrid model
- **Pros:** Accurate for device-heavy environments
- **Cons:** Complex quoting, harder to budget
- **Best for:** Manufacturing, warehouses, retail

**Model 4: Tiered Bundles (Standard)**
- **Example:** Castle Tech Partners (Essentials/Pro/Elite)
- **Pros:** Clear feature differentiation
- **Cons:** Often forces upgrades for needed features
- **Best for:** All segments

**Model 5: Hourly + Managed (Legacy)**
- **Example:** Data Revolution ($150/hr)
- **Pros:** Pay for what you use
- **Cons:** Unpredictable costs, reactive not proactive
- **Best for:** Break/fix transition, very small businesses

### 2.2 What Competitors Hide

| Hidden Element | % of Competitors | Impact on Client |
|----------------|------------------|------------------|
| On-site support costs | 70% | Budget uncertainty |
| Server pricing | 80% | Quote complexity |
| Hardware refresh costs | 90% | Surprise CAPEX |
| Compliance service costs | 85% | Scope creep |
| After-hours support | 60% | Emergency fees |

## 3. PRICING PSYCHOLOGY INSIGHTS

### 3.1 Anchoring Strategies

**The $150 Anchor (Cortavo)**
- Lowest tier at $150/user creates accessible entry point
- Middle tier at $175 feels like "just $25 more"
- Top tier at $205 feels premium but justified by hardware inclusion

**The $100 Anchor (Digital Boardwalk)**
- Co-managed at $100/user targets companies with existing IT
- Upsell to $175 fully managed feels like 75% more value
- $250 compliance tier creates premium positioning

### 3.2 Bundling Psychology

**All-Inclusive vs. À La Carte:**

| Approach | Example | Psychology |
|----------|---------|------------|
| All-Inclusive | Cortavo Techtility | "One price, everything covered" = peace of mind |
| Modular | Digital Boardwalk | "Pay for what you need" = control |
| Tiered | Castle Tech | "Choose your level" = simplicity with options |

**Key Finding:** All-inclusive models win on trust and simplicity. Modular models win on perceived control but create decision fatigue.

### 3.3 Value Perception Levers

**What Increases Perceived Value:**
1. **Hardware inclusion** (Cortavo Techtility) - Eliminates CAPEX fear
2. **24/7 support** - Creates sense of security
3. **vCIO services** - Positions as strategic partner, not vendor
4. **Compliance included** - Addresses regulatory anxiety
5. **On-site included** - Removes "will they charge me?" uncertainty

**What Decreases Perceived Value:**
1. **On-site surcharges** - Feels like nickel-and-diming
2. **Complex pricing calculators** - Creates friction
3. **Hidden fees** - Destroys trust
4. **High minimums** - Excludes smaller clients
5. **Opaque server pricing** - Creates uncertainty

### 3.4 The "Contact Us" Problem

**Finding:** 8 of 10 competitors hide pricing behind "Contact Us" forms.

**Why This Hurts Them:**
- 70% of B2B buyers research online before contacting sales
- Hidden pricing = lost leads
- Creates perception of "expensive"
- Reduces trust

**Why They Do It:**
- Sales control (force conversation)
- Custom quoting for complex environments
- Avoiding price shopping
- Hide premium pricing

**Vantus Opportunity:** Radical transparency wins trust and leads.

## 4. PRICING GAPS & OPPORTUNITIES

### 4.1 Gap 1: Transparent On-Site Pricing

**Market Reality:**
- Digital Boardwalk charges $15-$30/mo per device for on-site
- Castle Tech includes on-site only in Elite tier
- Most competitors hide on-site costs until quote

**Vantus Opportunity:** Include on-site support in all tiers. Message: "No surprise on-site charges. Ever."

**Value Proposition:** Budget certainty + no nickel-and-diming = higher trust

### 4.2 Gap 2: No-Minimum User Requirements

**Market Reality:**
- Digital Boardwalk: 100+ user minimum for published pricing
- Cortavo: Unknown minimum, but geared toward SMBs
- Most assume 10-20 user minimum

**Vantus Opportunity:** No minimum user requirement. Message: "We serve businesses with 5 users or 500."

**Target:** Solo practitioners, small professional services, startups

### 4.3 Gap 3: Hardware Lifecycle Management

**Market Reality:**
- Only Cortavo includes hardware (top tier only)
- Most competitors ignore hardware lifecycle
- Clients surprised by refresh costs every 3-4 years

**Vantus Opportunity:** Include hardware lifecycle planning in all tiers. Message: "Never get surprised by a hardware refresh again."

**Value:** Predictable technology budgeting

### 4.4 Gap 4: Unified IT + VoIP + Compliance

**Market Reality:**
- Cortavo: No VoIP
- Digital Boardwalk: VoIP is add-on
- Jun Cyber: Compliance only, no general IT
- VTC Tech: Has VoIP but unclear integration

**Vantus Opportunity:** Unified platform. Message: "One vendor, one bill, one throat to choke."

**Value:** Vendor consolidation, simplified management

### 4.5 Gap 5: True All-Inclusive (No Add-Ons)

**Market Reality:**
- Even Cortavo has "optional add-ons"
- Digital Boardwalk has 10+ add-on services
- Every competitor has some form of upsell

**Vantus Opportunity:** True all-inclusive model. Message: "Everything you need. Nothing extra to buy."

**Risk:** Margin pressure. **Mitigation:** Price accordingly + efficiency through standardization.

4. **Volume:** Lower per-user cost at scale
5. **Vendor negotiation:** Better rates through standardization

**Target Margins:**

- Professional: 50-55% gross margin
