| Wrong people get detailed info | Right information to right people |

## Communication Rules

### Our Communication Principles

1. **Plain Language**
   - No jargon without explanation
   - If you don't understand it, we didn't explain it well

2. **Timeliness**
   - Bad news doesn't get better with age
   - Share what we know when we know it
   - It's okay to say "we're still investigating"

3. **Relevance**
   - Right information to right people
   - Don't overwhelm with details
   - Highlight what matters

4. **Honesty**
   - Share the real situation
   - Admit what we don't know
   - Be clear about impact
