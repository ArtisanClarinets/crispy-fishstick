## Best Practices for Submitting Requests

- Be specific about what you need
- Explain the business reason
- Include all relevant details
- Identify who can approve
- Attach supporting documents
- Set realistic deadlines
- Be available for questions

---

## What Happens After You Submit

1. **Acknowledgment** (within 4 hours)
   - You receive confirmation with request ID

2. **Triage** (within 1 business day)
   - We classify and prioritize your request

3. **Response** (based on priority)
   - Critical: 4 hours
   - High: 1 day
   - Medium: 3 days
   - Low: 1 week

4. **Work begins**
   - You receive schedule and updates

5. **Completion**
   - You approve and we close the request
