Use this template to keep scope, evidence, approvals, and client ownership clear.
Do not add unsupported competitor, benchmark, or market-rank claims without dated source support.

---

## What This Template Is

A record of important decisions made about your IT—what was decided, why, and what other options were considered. Prevents revisiting the same decisions and helps explain choices to new team members.

---

## Why Decision Logging Matters

| Without Decision Log | With Decision Log |
|---------------------|-------------------|
| Forget why choices were made | Clear reasoning documented |
| Repeat discussions | Decisions stand |
| New people confused | Onboarding easier |
| No accountability | Clear ownership |
| Revisit same issues | Learn and move on |

| **Trade-offs** | Speed vs. cost |
| **Escalations** | Disagreements resolved |
| **Risk acceptance** | Deciding not to fix something |

## Best Practices

- Log immediately after decision
- Include why, not just what
- Document rejected options
- Note who was involved
- Review annually for patterns
- Link to related documents
- Be honest about trade-offs
