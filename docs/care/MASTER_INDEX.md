## What Vantus Care is

Vantus Care is the recurring support layer that follows project delivery.
It is designed for clients who want ownership, visibility, and a predictable operating rhythm after launch.

Care covers recurring support, reporting, operational hygiene, and clear service boundaries.
If the work needs new scope, significant change, or non-routine delivery effort, it moves into a scoped project or change request.

## How Care aligns to `enterprise_pack`

Vantus leads with project work and uses Care as the follow-on support layer when continuity is justified.

Default alignment:

- `Website Rebuild` -> `Foundation`
- `Website + CMS` -> `Advanced`
- `Website + Business Portal` -> `Sovereign`

Use `CARE_BUILD_TO_CARE_CROSSWALK.md` and `../enterprise_pack/03_offers/VANTUS_CARE_ALIGNMENT.md` for the canonical bridge.
