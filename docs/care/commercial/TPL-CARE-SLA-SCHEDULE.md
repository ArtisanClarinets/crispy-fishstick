**Type:** Commercial schedule template
**Status:** Baseline template

## Purpose

Use this schedule to define support windows, severity levels, response targets, and service boundaries for a specific Care client.

## Severity model

| Severity | Meaning | Example |
|---|---|---|
| SEV-1 | Critical business outage | Revenue-critical portal down |
| SEV-2 | Major degradation | Core workflow materially impaired |
| SEV-3 | Standard issue | Non-critical service problem |
| SEV-4 | Routine request | Low-risk service request |

## Exclusions

- New project work
- Major re-architecture without approved scope
- Hardware leasing or financing
- Legal, regulatory, or certification guarantees

## Client responsibilities

- Provide timely approvals and named points of contact.
- Maintain licensing and vendor relationships unless explicitly delegated.
- Preserve ownership and access records needed for support.
- Participate in reporting and governance meetings as agreed.
