## Included recurring services

The recurring service may include, as applicable:

- routine administration,
- support request coordination,
- monitoring and alert review,
- recurring reporting,
- vendor coordination,
- standard changes within agreed limits,
- governance and planning check-ins.

## Support ownership model

- Vantus owns coordination of recurring support activity within scope.
- The Client retains ownership of systems, accounts, data, licenses, hardware, and credentials.
- Vantus does not lease, rent, finance, or bundle hardware to the Client.
- Vantus works as the Client's support lead, not as the owner of the Client's assets.

## Scope boundaries

This proposal is for recurring operational support.
The following are not included unless separately approved in writing:

- project work,
- migrations,
- net-new integrations,
- custom development,
- major remediation,
- major reconfiguration,
- compliance certification work,
- hardware leasing, financing, or bundled device subscriptions.

## Assumptions

Pricing assumes a stable operating environment after any transition-in or project delivery period.
Pricing also assumes:

- the Client maintains required vendor licensing and subscriptions,
- the Client provides timely approvals and access,
- recurring work remains within normal administrative complexity,
- project-based work will be quoted separately if needed.

## Client responsibilities

To support delivery, the Client will:

- name business and technical contacts,
- maintain vendor and platform access,
- keep licenses and third-party contracts active,
- make timely decisions and approvals,
- participate in agreed reporting or review meetings.
