# CARE_BUILD_TO_CARE_CROSSWALK

**Purpose:** Show how the primary Vantus build offers convert into Vantus Care.
**Last updated:** 2026-03-07
**Classification:** Internal - sales and delivery alignment
**Publication rule:** Internal guide; convert to client-facing language before external use.

## Rule

Vantus sells project work first and Care second.
Care is the recurring operating layer that protects what was delivered.

## Build-to-care mapping

| Enterprise-pack offer | Typical post-launch need | Default Care recommendation |
|---|---|---|
| Website Rebuild | hosting oversight, updates, backups, monitoring, minor support | Foundation |
| Website + CMS | content operations, release discipline, stronger reporting, role governance | Advanced |
| Website + Business Portal | uptime governance, change control, lifecycle planning, higher-touch support | Sovereign |

## When to upgrade care tier

Move a client up-tier when one or more of the following becomes true:

- more stakeholders need governance reporting,
- the system carries operational or revenue-critical workflows,
- compliance-support or evidence requests become regular,
- after-hours support expectations increase,
- change control and risk planning become more formal.

## When Care is not the right next step

Do not force Care when the client only needs:

- one-time bug fixes,
- a short stabilization engagement,
- a standalone project change order,
- unmanaged hosting with no governance expectations.

## Sales guidance

- Lead with the build package outcome.
- Position Care as protection, continuity, and operating discipline.
- Keep recurring scope separate from project backlog.
- Use the Care tier that matches risk, cadence, and governance needs - not just ticket volume.

## Delivery guidance

- Convert launch artifacts into Care onboarding inputs.
- Carry over the owner handoff package, credentials map, monitoring baseline, and rollback posture.
- Open a change register if recurring support exposes deferred project work.
