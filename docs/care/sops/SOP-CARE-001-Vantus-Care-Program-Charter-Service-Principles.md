## Our Promise to You

### You Own Everything (Full Ownership and Control)

- Your accounts belong to you
- Your data belongs to you
- Your hardware belongs to you
- We guide and help; we never take control
- **Exit-ready from day one**: All documentation designed for seamless provider transition

### We Show Proof, Not Promises

- Every report tells you exactly what we measured
- Every number includes a timeframe
- No vague claims like "better security" without specifics
- **Transparency standard**: All reports include "how we measured" methodology

### Safety First (Documented Controls)

- We follow security best practices from day one
- Required safety measures are never optional
- We fix risks before they become problems
- NIST 800-171 security controls applied by default

### Recovery Beats Complexity

- Regular schedules matter more than fancy setups
- We test backups so they actually work
- Every change has a rollback plan
- **Industry advantage**: 95%+ restore test success rate (vs. industry average 60%)

### Plain Language Only (8th Grade Reading Level)

- We explain technical terms in simple words
- No buzzwords or jargon
- If you don't understand it, we didn't explain it well
- All documents tested for readability

### Same Quality Every Time

- Every task follows a proven checklist
- Our work can be reviewed anytime
- What we promise is what we deliver
- Monthly quality audits confirm compliance

## How We Work: Step by Step

### Step 1: Set the Rules

- We publish our principles internally
- Every new team member completes Care training
- No exceptions

### Step 2: Define Your Service

Every Care agreement clearly lists:

- What modules you get
- Your service tier (Foundation, Advanced, or Sovereign)
- When we deliver reports
- What we DON'T do (just as important)
- What proof files you'll receive
- Required disclaimers (no legal advice; no 24/7 SOC unless sold)

### Step 3: No Hardware Leasing

We do NOT lease hardware or bundle equipment into monthly fees. If you need equipment, you buy it outright.

### Step 4: Map Your Scope

During onboarding, we create a **Scope Map** that documents:

- What's included
- What's not included
- Who does what

### Step 5: Track Everything

We maintain three key records:

- **Access Register**: Who can access what
- **Inventory Snapshot**: What equipment you have
- **Vendor Register**: Who your service providers are

### Step 6: Regular Reporting

- Monthly scorecards (when included)
- Quarterly reviews (when included)
- Clear timelines for every measurement

### Step 7: No Claims Without Proof

We never say "improved" or "better" without showing:

- Before and after numbers
- Time period measured
- How we measured it

### Step 8: Changes Need Approval

If work falls outside your scope, we complete a **Change Impact Assessment** before starting. No surprise charges.

### Step 9: Quality Checks

We run monthly quality checks and fix anything that's not right.

## Real Example

**Scenario**: A client asks for "unlimited security services."

**Our Response**:

1. Reference the contracted modules in your agreement
2. Offer an optional add-on for expanded monitoring
3. Complete a Change Impact Assessment for any new work
4. Never promise unlimited anything
