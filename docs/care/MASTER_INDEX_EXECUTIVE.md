## Executive summary

Vantus Care is the recurring support and governance layer that follows Vantus project delivery.
Its purpose is to protect client ownership, provide predictable support, and create a disciplined operating rhythm after launch.

The suite is now organized around five executive questions:

1. What is Care?
2. How does Care fit the Vantus strategy?
3. How is Care packaged and priced?
4. What commercial controls protect margin and scope?
5. What operating system supports delivery quality?
