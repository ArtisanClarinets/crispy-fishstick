# Master Acceptance Criteria

## Scope and architecture

- Public site work is completed in `apps/web`.
- Non-public apps are untouched for feature implementation.
- Route contract is complete and consistent with public core specs.

## Visual and UX quality

- Design quality is intentional and premium.
- Desktop, tablet, and mobile experiences are fully supported.
- Mobile interaction quality feels native-app-like.
- Brand component audit checklist is complete.

## Content and pricing integrity

- Offer language and pricing values match canonical sources.
- No placeholders or ad-hoc pricing edits exist.
- Public-safe copy boundaries are maintained.

## Engineering quality

- Security baseline, performance baseline, accessibility baseline, SEO baseline, and observability baseline are all met.
- Lint, type-check, build, and required test suites pass.

## Launch readiness

- Release and rollback procedures are documented.
- Post-release smoke checks and stabilization plan are documented.
