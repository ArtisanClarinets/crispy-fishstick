# Copy Brief

Version: 2.0
Last updated: 2026-03-06
Scope: public website copy system

## Voice

- Direct, calm, plain language.
- Proof first, hype never.
- Explain tradeoffs without vague superlatives.

## Mandatory public claims

- Service positioning follows `../../enterprise_pack/03_offers/SERVICE_CATALOG_PUBLIC.md`.
- Pricing statements follow `../../pricing/pricing_public.yaml` v2.0 values.
- If a metric is mentioned, include method or evidence context.

## Pricing copy rules

- Use "starting at" and "typical range" language only.
- Include: "Final pricing is confirmed after discovery and written scope."
- Do not include internal discount or margin policy details.

## Core page messaging

- Home: what Vantus delivers, who it is for, what to do next.
- Services: the three public offers and what is included.
- Pricing: anchors and ranges for offers, infrastructure, and add-ons.
- Proof: evidence-backed outcomes and case study context.
