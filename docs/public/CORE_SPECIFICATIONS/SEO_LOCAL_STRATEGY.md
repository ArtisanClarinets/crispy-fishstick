# SEO and Local Discovery Strategy

Version: 2.0
Last updated: 2026-03-06
Scope: public routes in `apps/public`

## Goal

Capture high-intent service demand and convert to qualified inquiry flows.

## Content strategy

- Prioritize service intent pages and proof-linked content.
- Keep location-aware content factual and non-templated.
- Use case studies and proof artifacts to support authority claims.

## Technical SEO requirements

- `sitemap.xml` and `robots.txt` must be generated and accurate.
- Canonical tags must resolve correctly on dynamic routes.
- Structured data must be valid for org/service/article contexts.
- Core Web Vitals targets must remain within release quality gates.

## Measurement baseline

- Organic impressions and clicks per route cluster.
- Conversion events from `/start-audit` and `/contact`.
- Engagement and conversion trends on `/services`, `/pricing`, and `/proof`.
